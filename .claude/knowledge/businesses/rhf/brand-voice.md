# Red Hill Fresh - Brand Voice

## Voice Characteristics

### We Are
- **Friendly** - Like talking to a neighbor
- **Local** - Proud of the Peninsula
- **Passionate** - We love good produce
- **Honest** - No supermarket spin
- **Helpful** - Here to make your life easier

### We Are NOT
- Corporate or formal
- Pushy or salesy
- Generic or bland
- Pretentious or exclusive
- Impersonal

## Tone by Channel

### Email
- Warm and friendly
- Like a note from a friend
- Personal touches welcome
- Enthusiasm for seasonal produce

### Social Media
- Casual and conversational
- Beautiful food photography
- Local references and stories
- Community engagement

### Website
- Clear and helpful
- Warm but professional
- Easy to understand
- Focus on freshness and quality

### Customer Service
- Patient and understanding
- Solution-focused
- Personal (use names)
- Local knowledge

## Language Guidelines

### Words We Use
- Fresh, local, quality
- Neighbours, community
- Hand-picked, carefully sourced
- Family-run, small-batch
- Seasonal, in-season
- Peninsula, local farms

### Words We Avoid
- Cheap, budget, discount (we're premium)
- Corporate jargon
- "Click here" (be more natural)
- Overly formal language
- Generic marketing speak

## Example Copy

### Good
> "This week's strawberries are absolutely stunning - sweet, juicy, and perfect for summer. Grab them before they're gone!"

### Not Good
> "Our premium strawberry product offering is now available for purchase through our e-commerce platform."

### Good
> "Hey Peninsula! The asparagus season has finally arrived. We've got fresh spears from a farm just down the road in Main Ridge."

### Not Good
> "Dear Valued Customer, We are pleased to announce the commencement of asparagus season."
