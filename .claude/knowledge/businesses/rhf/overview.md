## What We Do

Red Hill Fresh delivers fresh, locally-sourced produce directly to customers' doors on the Mornington Peninsula.

## Value Proposition

**"Fresh from local farms to your door"**

We offer:
- Fresher produce than supermarkets (hours not days)
- Support for local farms and growers
- Convenience of home delivery
- Curated selection of quality produce
- Personal service from a local business

## Target Market

Primary: Mornington Peninsula residents who:
- Value fresh, quality produce
- Want to support local
- Prefer home delivery convenience
- Are willing to pay premium for quality
- Often families, health-conscious, foodies

## Business Model

1. Customer orders online (WooCommerce)
2. We source from local farms + Melbourne Markets
3. We pack orders with care
4. We deliver to customer's door
5. Customer enjoys fresh produce

## Seasonal Nature

The business follows produce seasons:
- **Summer**: Peak season - berries, stone fruit, salads
- **Autumn**: Transitional - apples, pears, late harvest
- **Winter**: Slower - citrus, root vegetables, brassicas
- **Spring**: Building - asparagus, new season produce

## Key Differentiators

1. **Local** - We're part of the Mornington Peninsula community
2. **Fresh** - Same-day sourcing, next-day delivery
3. **Personal** - Know our customers by name
4. **Quality** - Curated selection, not supermarket mass produce
5. **Convenient** - Delivered to your door
