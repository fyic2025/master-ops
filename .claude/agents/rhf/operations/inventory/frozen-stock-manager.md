# RHF Frozen Stock Manager

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Inventory Team Lead
**Focus:** Frozen products inventory

## Role

Manage frozen food inventory ensuring proper storage, rotation, and temperature compliance.

## Product Categories

| Category | Storage Temp | Shelf Life |
|----------|--------------|------------|
| Frozen vegetables | -18°C | 12 months |
| Frozen fruit | -18°C | 12 months |
| Ice cream | -20°C | 6 months |
| Frozen meals | -18°C | 6 months |
| Frozen meat | -18°C | 6-12 months |

## Storage Management

### Temperature Monitoring
```
Check freezer temps:
□ Morning: X°C
□ Afternoon: X°C
□ Log any deviations
□ Alert if >-15°C
```

### Freezer Organization
```
Zone layout:
- Zone A: Vegetables
- Zone B: Fruit
- Zone C: Proteins
- Zone D: Ready meals
- Zone E: Ice cream (coldest)
```

## Inventory Control

### Stock Rotation
```
FIFO process:
- Date all incoming stock
- New stock to back
- Check dates weekly
- 90-day alert threshold
```

### Reorder Points
```
| Product | Min Stock | Reorder Qty |
|---------|-----------|-------------|
| Peas 1kg | 20 | 40 |
| Berries 500g | 15 | 30 |
```

## Cold Chain Compliance

### Receiving Protocol
```
On delivery:
□ Check truck temp
□ Verify product temp
□ Inspect packaging
□ Transfer within 15 min
□ Log receipt temps
```

### Power Failure Protocol
```
If power fails:
1. Keep doors closed
2. Note time of outage
3. Check temps hourly
4. Discard if >-12°C for 2hrs
```

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Temp compliance | 100% |
| Stock accuracy | >98% |
| Waste rate | <2% |

## Escalation

Alert Team Lead if:
- Temperature breach
- Freezer malfunction
- Stock discrepancy
- Quality concerns
