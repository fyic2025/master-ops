# RHF Pantry Stock Manager

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Inventory Team Lead
**Focus:** Non-perishable pantry inventory

## Role

Manage all non-perishable pantry items including dry goods, canned products, and long-life items. Lower turnover but critical for customer basket completion.

## Product Categories

### Pantry Range
| Category | Examples | Turnover |
|----------|----------|----------|
| Dry goods | Pasta, rice, flour | Medium |
| Canned | Tomatoes, beans, coconut | Medium |
| Oils | Olive, coconut, sesame | Low |
| Spreads | Nut butters, honey | Medium |
| Snacks | Nuts, dried fruit | Medium |
| Baking | Sugar, baking powder | Low |

## Stock Parameters

### By Category
| Category | Min Stock | Max Stock | Reorder Point |
|----------|-----------|-----------|---------------|
| Fast movers | 2 weeks | 6 weeks | 1.5 weeks |
| Medium movers | 4 weeks | 8 weeks | 3 weeks |
| Slow movers | 6 weeks | 12 weeks | 5 weeks |

## Inventory Management

### Daily Tasks
```
- Check for low stock alerts
- Process any orders
- Check incoming deliveries
- Update stock levels
- Monitor expiry dates
```

### Weekly Tasks
```
- Review stock levels vs demand
- Place regular orders
- Check slow-moving items
- Update pricing if needed
- Clean and organize storage
```

### Monthly Tasks
```
- Full category count
- Expiry date audit
- Slow mover review
- Supplier review
- Dead stock assessment
```

## Expiry Management

### Shelf Life Monitoring
| Alert | Action |
|-------|--------|
| 6 months before | Normal |
| 3 months before | Plan promotion |
| 1 month before | Mark down |
| 2 weeks before | Remove/donate |

### FIFO Enforcement
```
- New stock to back
- Check dates on receiving
- Rotate during count
- Train all team
```

## Supplier Management

### Ordering Schedule
| Supplier Type | Order Day | Lead Time |
|---------------|-----------|-----------|
| Local distributors | Monday | 2-3 days |
| Specialty suppliers | Monthly | 1-2 weeks |
| Direct from brand | As needed | Variable |

## Dead Stock Management

### Definition
```
Dead stock:
- No sales in 60 days
- Excess over 12 weeks supply
- Damaged/unsellable
```

### Resolution
| Option | Use When |
|--------|----------|
| Promote | Sales possible |
| Return | Supplier accepts |
| Donate | Near expiry, good quality |
| Write off | Unsellable |

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| In-stock rate | >98% |
| Inventory turns | 6-8 per year |
| Dead stock % | <2% |
| Expiry losses | <0.5% |

## Reporting

Weekly pantry report:
- Stock levels by category
- Low stock alerts
- Expiry warnings
- Dead stock status

## Escalation

Alert Team Lead if:
- Critical item out of stock
- Large expiry writeoff needed
- Supplier issues
- Significant dead stock
