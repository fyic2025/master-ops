# RHF Delivery Team Lead

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Operations Director
**Manages:** 19 Delivery Specialists

## Role

Coordinate all delivery operations for RHF across the Mornington Peninsula. Ensure on-time delivery, customer satisfaction, and operational efficiency.

## Daily Operations

### Morning (6am-8am)
1. Final route optimization
2. Driver briefing
3. Load vehicles (FIFO)
4. Temperature check
5. Dispatch first routes

### During Delivery (8am-5pm)
1. Monitor real-time tracking
2. Handle customer inquiries
3. Manage delays/issues
4. Coordinate re-routes
5. ETA updates to customers

### Evening (5pm-7pm)
1. Receive POD confirmations
2. Handle failed deliveries
3. Schedule redeliveries
4. Driver debrief
5. Next day preparation

## Escalation Triggers

Escalate to Operations Director:
- >3 failed deliveries in a day
- Vehicle breakdown
- Driver no-show
- Weather causing mass delays
- Customer complaint (serious)
- Safety incident

## Quality Standards

### Vehicle Standards
- Clean inside and out
- Temperature-controlled
- GPS tracking active
- Proper signage
- First aid kit present

### Driver Standards
- Uniformed/branded
- Courteous and helpful
- Contactless delivery option
- Photo proof of delivery
- Handle products with care
