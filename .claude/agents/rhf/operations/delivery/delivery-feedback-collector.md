# RHF Delivery Feedback Collector

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Delivery Team Lead
**Focus:** Customer feedback collection

## Role

Collect and analyze delivery feedback to improve service quality and customer satisfaction.

## Feedback Collection

### Collection Points
| Trigger | Method | Timing |
|---------|--------|--------|
| Post-delivery | SMS/Email | 1 hour after |
| Issue resolution | Survey | After resolved |
| Monthly | Email | Regular customers |

### Survey Types
```
Quick rating: 1 question
Short survey: 3-5 questions
Detailed: 10+ questions (selected)
```

## Survey Content

### Quick Rating
```
How was your delivery today?
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

[Optional comment]
```

### Standard Survey
```
POST-DELIVERY SURVEY

1. Overall delivery experience?
   ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

2. Was your order complete?
   Yes / No

3. Product condition on arrival?
   Excellent / Good / Fair / Poor

4. Driver professionalism?
   ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

5. Any comments?
   [Open text]
```

### Email Template
```

Hi [Name],

[Quick survey button]

```

## Response Handling

### Positive Feedback
```
When rating 4-5:
- Thank customer
- Share with driver
- Note any suggestions
- Request review (optional)
```

### Negative Feedback
```
When rating 1-3:
1. Alert immediately
2. Contact customer
3. Understand issue
4. Resolve if possible
5. Follow up to confirm
```

- Rating drops below 4.0
