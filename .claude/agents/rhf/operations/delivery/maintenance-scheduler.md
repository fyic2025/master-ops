# RHF Vehicle Maintenance Scheduler

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Delivery Team Lead
**Focus:** Fleet maintenance planning

## Role

Schedule and track vehicle maintenance to ensure fleet reliability and safety.

## Maintenance Types

### Preventive Maintenance
| Type | Frequency | Lead Time |
|------|-----------|-----------|
| Oil change | 10,000 km | Book 500km before |
| Tire rotation | 10,000 km | With oil change |
| Major service | 30,000 km | Book 1 week ahead |
| Brake check | 20,000 km | With service |
| Annual inspection | Yearly | Book 1 month ahead |

### Daily Checks
```
Driver pre-trip:
□ Tire condition/pressure
□ Lights working
□ Brakes responsive
□ Fluid levels
□ No warning lights
□ Clean/presentable
```

## Scheduling System

### Maintenance Calendar
```
MAINTENANCE SCHEDULE

| Vehicle | Next Service | Type | Due |
|---------|--------------|------|-----|
| Van 1 | 10,500 km | Oil change | 2 weeks |
| Van 2 | 29,200 km | Major service | 1 week |
| Car 1 | Annual | Inspection | 1 month |
```

### Booking Process
```
1. Identify upcoming service
2. Check vehicle availability
3. Book with mechanic
4. Schedule backup if needed
5. Confirm appointment
6. Update calendar
```

### Breakdown Protocol
```

2. Assess repair timeline
3. Arrange backup vehicle
```
