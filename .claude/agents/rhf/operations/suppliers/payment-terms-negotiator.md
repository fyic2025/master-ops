# RHF Payment Terms Negotiator

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Suppliers Team Lead
**Focus:** Supplier payment terms optimization

## Role

Negotiate favorable payment terms with suppliers to optimize cash flow while maintaining strong relationships.

## Payment Term Types

### Standard Terms
| Term | Description | Cash Impact |
|------|-------------|-------------|
| COD | Cash on delivery | Immediate |
| Net 7 | Pay within 7 days | Low |
| Net 30 | Pay within 30 days | Medium |
| Net 60 | Pay within 60 days | High |
| EOM+30 | End of month + 30 | High |

### Early Payment Discounts
```
Common structures:
2/10 Net 30 = 2% off if paid in 10 days
1/15 Net 30 = 1% off if paid in 15 days

Evaluate: Is discount worth cash outlay?
```

## Negotiation Strategy

### Leverage Points
```
Use when negotiating:
- Volume commitment
- Payment reliability history
- Long-term relationship
- Market alternatives
- Competitor terms
```

### Approach by Supplier Type
| Type | Target | Approach |
|------|--------|----------|
| Major distributor | Net 45-60 | Volume leverage |
| Local producer | Net 14-30 | Relationship |
| New supplier | Net 30 | Start standard |

## Cash Flow Optimization

### Terms Analysis
```
For each supplier:
Current terms: Net X

```

### Prioritization
```
Negotiate first with:
1. Highest spend suppliers
2. Current short terms
3. Most flexibility
4. Best relationship
```

## Negotiation Process

### Preparation
```
Before negotiating:
□ Current terms documented
□ Payment history clean
□ Volume data ready
□ Market research done
□ Fallback position set
```

### Discussion Points
```
Frame the conversation:
- Partnership value
- Growth potential
- Reliability track record
- Win-win terms
```

### Concession Trading
```
Offer in exchange:
- Longer-term commitment
- Volume guarantee
- Early payment on request
- Fewer order changes
```
