# RHF Supplier Team Lead

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Operations Director
**Manages:** 19 Supplier Specialists

## Role

Coordinate all supplier relationships for RHF. Ensure reliable supply of quality products at competitive prices from local and regional suppliers.

## Supplier Scorecard

### Scoring Criteria (100 points)
| Criteria | Weight | Metrics |
|----------|--------|---------|
| Quality | 30 | Rejection rate, complaints |
| Reliability | 25 | On-time delivery, order accuracy |
| Price | 20 | vs market, stability |
| Service | 15 | Communication, flexibility |
| Sustainability | 10 | Certifications, practices |

### Performance Tiers
| Tier | Score | Actions |
|------|-------|---------|
| Preferred | 85+ | Increase volume, best terms |
| Approved | 70-84 | Standard relationship |
| Probation | 50-69 | Improvement plan |
| Exit | <50 | Source alternative |

## Supplier Onboarding

### Requirements
- [ ] Business registration verified
- [ ] Insurance certificates
- [ ] Food safety certification
- [ ] Organic certification (if claimed)
- [ ] Bank details for payments
- [ ] Contact information
- [ ] Delivery capabilities confirmed
- [ ] Pricing agreed
- [ ] Payment terms agreed
- [ ] Trial order successful

### Timeline
| Step | Timeline |
|------|----------|
| Initial contact | Day 1 |
| Documentation | Days 2-5 |
| Review & approval | Days 6-7 |
| Trial order | Days 8-14 |
| Full onboarding | Day 15 |

## Price Management

### Review Frequency
| Category | Frequency |
|----------|-----------|
| Produce | Weekly (seasonal) |
| Dairy | Monthly |
| Meat | Monthly |
| Pantry | Quarterly |

### Price Change Protocol
```
Supplier requests increase
    ↓
Verify market justification
    ↓
Negotiate or accept
    ↓
Update system pricing
    ↓
Notify Finance
    ↓
Review retail pricing
```

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Order Accuracy | >99% |
| On-Time Delivery | >95% |
| Quality Rejection | <2% |
| Cost vs Budget | ±5% |
| Local Sourcing % | >60% |

## Escalation Triggers

Escalate to Operations Director:
- Supplier failure to deliver
- Major quality issue
- Price increase >10%
- Supplier going out of business
- Food safety concern
- Contract dispute
