# RHF Dairy Supplier Manager

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Suppliers Team Lead
**Focus:** Dairy supplier relationships

## Role

Manage relationships with dairy suppliers to ensure consistent supply of fresh, quality dairy products.

## Supplier Portfolio

### Key Suppliers
| Supplier | Products | Delivery |
|----------|----------|----------|
| Local dairy farms | Fresh milk, cream | Daily |
| Cheese producers | Artisan cheese | 2x week |
| Yogurt makers | Yogurt, kefir | 3x week |
| Butter suppliers | Butter, spreads | Weekly |

```

- Planning ahead
```

### Supplier Meetings
```
Quarterly agenda:
1. Performance review

4. Forecast sharing
5. Improvement areas
```

## Order Management

### Daily Orders
```
Milk ordering:
- Order by 2pm for next day
- Based on forecast + actuals
- Confirm delivery time
- Note any specials
```

### Lead Times
| Product | Lead Time | Min Order |
|---------|-----------|-----------|
| Fresh milk | 1 day | 50L |
| Cream | 1 day | 10L |
| Cheese | 3 days | 5kg |
| Yogurt | 2 days | 20 units |

## Quality Standards

### Dairy Requirements
```
All dairy must:
- Maintain cold chain (<4°C)
- Have 7+ days shelf life
- Meet food safety standards
- Be properly labeled
- Arrive in good condition
```

### Quality Issues
```
If quality issue:
1. Document with photos
2. Notify supplier same day
3. Request credit/replacement
4. Track resolution
5. Update quality log
```

## Cold Chain

### Temperature Critical
```
Monitor:
- Delivery truck temp
- Product temp on arrival
- Receiving area temp
- Storage immediately

Reject if:
- Temp >5°C on arrival
- Packaging damaged
- Past best practice date
```
