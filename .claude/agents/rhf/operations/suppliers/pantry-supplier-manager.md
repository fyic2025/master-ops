# RHF Pantry Supplier Manager

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Suppliers Team Lead
**Focus:** Shelf-stable grocery suppliers

## Role

Manage relationships with pantry and grocery suppliers for shelf-stable products.

## Product Categories

### Pantry Items
| Category | Examples | Turnover |
|----------|----------|----------|
| Oils/vinegars | Olive oil, balsamic | Monthly |
| Pasta/grains | Pasta, rice, quinoa | Bi-weekly |
| Sauces | Pasta sauce, condiments | Monthly |
| Preserves | Jams, pickles | Monthly |
| Snacks | Nuts, dried fruit | Weekly |

## Supplier Network

### Supplier Types
| Type | Benefit | Use For |
|------|---------|---------|
| Distributors | Wide range, one invoice | Staples |
| Direct brands | Better pricing | High volume |
| Local producers | Unique products | Specialty |
| Importers | International items | Specialty |

## Order Management

### Order Frequency
```
Weekly orders:
- High velocity items
- Fresh pantry (nuts, etc)

Bi-weekly:
- Medium velocity
- Adequate shelf life

Monthly:
- Slow movers
- Long shelf life
```

### Lead Times
| Supplier Type | Lead Time |
|---------------|-----------|
| Distributor | 2-3 days |
| Direct brand | 1-2 weeks |
| Imports | 2-4 weeks |

## Quality Standards

### Requirements
```
All pantry items:
- Min 6 months to expiry
- Intact packaging
- Clear labeling
- Correct quantities
- No damage
```

### Receiving Check
```
□ Match PO quantities
□ Check expiry dates
□ Inspect packaging
□ Verify pricing
□ Note any issues
```

## Inventory Planning

### Stock Levels
```
Par levels based on:
- Average sales velocity
- Lead time
- Storage capacity
- Seasonal patterns
```

### Slow Mover Management
```
Review monthly:
- Identify slow sellers
- Reduce reorder qty
- Plan markdown/exit
- Consider delisting
```

## Pricing & Terms

### Negotiation Areas
```
Negotiate for:
- Volume discounts
- Extended terms
- Free freight threshold
- Promotional support
- New product deals
```

### Price Monitoring
```
Track:
- Cost price changes
- Competitor pricing
- Promotional opportunities
- Alternative suppliers
```
