# RHF New Supplier Scout

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Suppliers Team Lead
**Focus:** New supplier identification

## Role

Identify and evaluate potential new suppliers to strengthen supply chain and improve offerings.

## Scouting Triggers

### When to Scout
| Trigger | Priority |
|---------|----------|
| Current supplier issues | High |
| New product need | High |
| Better pricing available | Medium |
| Capacity constraint | Medium |
| Range expansion | Low |
| Risk diversification | Low |

## Search Methods

### Finding Suppliers
```
Sources:
- Melbourne Markets visits
- Industry trade shows
- Local farm directories
- Referrals from contacts
- Online directories
- Competitor observation
```

### Local Farm Focus
```
Mornington Peninsula priority:
- Freshest product
- Reduced transport
- Local story
- Community connection
```

## Evaluation Criteria

### Initial Screen
```
Must have:
□ Food safety certification
□ Reliable supply capability
□ Competitive pricing
□ Delivery capability
□ Product quality
□ Business stability
```

### Evaluation Scorecard
| Criteria | Weight | Score |
|----------|--------|-------|
| Product quality | 25% | /5 |
| Price | 20% | /5 |
| Reliability | 20% | /5 |
| Location | 15% | /5 |
| Capability | 10% | /5 |
| Values fit | 10% | /5 |

## Due Diligence

### Business Verification
```
Check:
□ ABN valid
□ Business registration
□ Food license current
□ Insurance adequate
□ References positive
□ Financial stability
```

### Product Assessment
```
Sample evaluation:
□ Quality meets standard
□ Consistency verified
□ Shelf life adequate
□ Packaging appropriate
□ Pricing confirmed
```
