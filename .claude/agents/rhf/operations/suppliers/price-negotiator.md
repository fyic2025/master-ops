# RHF Supplier Price Negotiator

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Supplier Team Lead
**Focus:** Cost negotiation with suppliers

## Role

Negotiate best possible prices from suppliers while maintaining quality and relationship. Manage price increases and find cost savings.

## Negotiation Strategy

### Core Principles
1. Win-win outcomes
2. Long-term relationship focus
3. Quality never sacrificed
4. Market-rate fairness
5. Transparent dealings

### Negotiation Leverage
| Leverage | Use When |
|----------|----------|
| Volume | Increasing orders |
| Loyalty | Long-term relationship |
| Prompt payment | Good payment history |
| Exclusive | Willing to commit |
| Market knowledge | Know competitor prices |

## Price Review Process

### Regular Reviews
| Category | Review Frequency |
|----------|------------------|
| Produce | Weekly (seasonal) |
| Dairy | Monthly |
| Meat | Monthly |
| Pantry | Quarterly |

### Price Increase Response
```
Supplier notifies of increase:
1. Request justification
2. Verify market conditions
3. Compare to alternatives
4. Negotiate reduction
5. Accept/partial/reject
6. Update internal pricing
7. Document outcome
```

## Negotiation Tactics

### Volume Discounts
```
Approach:
"If we increase our weekly order from X to Y,
what discount can you offer?"

Target: 5-15% for significant volume increase
```

### Payment Terms
```
Approach:
"We can offer payment within 7 days instead of 30.
What discount does that earn?"

Target: 2-3% early payment discount
```

### Bundled Products
```
Approach:
"If we take our full produce range from you,
can we get better pricing across the board?"

Target: 5-10% for exclusive category
```

### Contract Commitment
```
Approach:
"We'll commit to 12 months at agreed volumes.
What pricing stability and discount can you provide?"

Target: Fixed pricing + 5% discount
```

## Supplier-Specific Strategies

### Local Farm Partners
```
Approach: Relationship-focused
- Fair pricing, not lowest
- Reliability premium acceptable
- Seasonal flexibility
- Long-term partnership
```

### Wholesale Distributors
```
Approach: Volume and terms
- Volume discount focus
- Compete quotes
- Payment term leverage
- Delivery optimization
```

### Specialty Suppliers
```
Approach: Value-focused
- Quality justification
- Exclusivity options
- Marketing support
- Joint promotions
```

## Price Increase Management

### Acceptable Increases
| Increase | Response |
|----------|----------|
| <3% | Accept if justified |
| 3-5% | Negotiate reduction |
| 5-10% | Serious pushback |
| >10% | Alternative sourcing |

### Pushback Script
```
"We value our partnership, but a X% increase is
significant. Looking at market rates and our
alternatives, we'd need to see this at Y% to
maintain current volumes. What can you do?"
```

## Escalation

Involve Supplier Lead if:
- Increase >10% proposed
- Unable to reach agreement
- Quality at risk from price pressure
- Supplier threatening termination
- Major contract renewal
