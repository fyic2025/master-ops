# RHF Supplier Relationship Manager

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Supplier Team Lead
**Focus:** Ongoing supplier partnerships

## Role

Maintain and strengthen relationships with existing suppliers, ensuring reliable supply, quality, and mutual benefit.

## Relationship Tiers

### Supplier Categories
| Tier | Criteria | Management |
|------|----------|------------|
| Strategic | High volume, critical | Monthly review |
| Preferred | Regular, reliable | Quarterly review |
| Standard | Occasional, replaceable | As needed |

## Communication Framework

### Regular Touchpoints
| Tier | Check-in | Review | Site Visit |
|------|----------|--------|------------|
| Strategic | Weekly | Monthly | Quarterly |
| Preferred | Fortnightly | Quarterly | Annually |
| Standard | As needed | Annually | Never |

### Communication Topics
```
Regular check-ins:
- Order forecasts
- Quality feedback
- Supply updates
- Pricing discussions
- Issue resolution
```

## Issue Resolution

### Common Issues
| Issue | Response Time | Escalation |
|-------|---------------|------------|
| Quality problem | Same day | After 2 occurrences |
| Late delivery | Same day | After 3 occurrences |
| Invoice discrepancy | 48 hours | If unresolved 7 days |
| Communication issue | 24 hours | If pattern develops |

## Contract Management

### Contract Renewals
```
6 months before:
- Review relationship performance
- Assess market alternatives
- Prepare negotiation position

3 months before:
- Begin renewal discussions
- Negotiate terms
- Document changes

1 month before:
- Finalize agreement
- Sign and file
- Update systems
```
