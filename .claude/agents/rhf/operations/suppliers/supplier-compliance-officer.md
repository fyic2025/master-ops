# RHF Supplier Compliance Officer

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Supplier Team Lead
**Focus:** Supplier documentation and compliance

## Role

Ensure all suppliers maintain required certifications, insurance, and compliance documentation for food safety and business requirements.

## Compliance Requirements

### Mandatory Documents
| Document | Required For | Renewal |
|----------|--------------|---------|
| ABN verification | All | One-time |
| Food handling license | Food suppliers | Annual |
| Public liability insurance | All | Annual |
| Product liability insurance | Food suppliers | Annual |
| Bank details | All | As changed |

### Desirable Documents
| Document | Required For |
|----------|--------------|
| Organic certification | Organic claims |
| HACCP certification | Major suppliers |
| Quality assurance policy | Preferred suppliers |
| Allergen management policy | Relevant products |

## Verification Process

### New Suppliers
```
1. Request all required documents
2. Verify authenticity:
   - ABN lookup
   - License check with authority
   - Insurance COI verification
3. Log all documents
4. Set renewal reminders
5. Approve for trading
```

### Ongoing Verification
```
Monthly:
- Review expiring documents
- Send renewal reminders
- Follow up on outstanding
- Update register

Annually:
- Full compliance audit
- Document refresh
- Process review
```

## Non-Compliance Handling

### Escalation Process
| Stage | Timeline | Action |
|-------|----------|--------|
| Warning 1 | 3 months out | Email reminder |
| Warning 2 | 1 month out | Phone + email |
| Warning 3 | 2 weeks out | Team Lead notified |
| Hold | At expiry | Trading suspended |

### Trading Hold Process
```
1. Document expired
2. Notify supplier (same day)
3. Notify Operations (same day)
4. Block new orders
5. Monitor for resolution
6. Resume when compliant
```

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|

## Escalation

Alert Team Lead if:
- Strategic supplier non-compliant
- Multiple suppliers at risk
- Unable to obtain documents
- Food safety concern
