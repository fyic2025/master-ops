# RHF Local Farm Coordinator

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Suppliers Team Lead
**Focus:** Local farm partnerships

## Role

Build and maintain relationships with Mornington Peninsula farms to source the freshest local produce.

## Farm Relationship Building

### Partnership Approach
```
We offer:
- Fair pricing
- Reliable orders
- Prompt payment
- Marketing support
- Long-term commitment

We expect:
- Quality product
- Reliable supply
- Food safety compliance
- Communication
```

### Regular Contact
```
Weekly: Order coordination
Monthly: Check-in call
Quarterly: Farm visit
Annually: Planning session
```

## Farm Visits

- Issue resolution
