# RHF Backup Supplier Manager

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Suppliers Team Lead
**Focus:** Contingency supplier network

## Role

Maintain a network of backup suppliers to ensure business continuity when primary suppliers cannot deliver.

## Backup Strategy

### Coverage Goals
```
Every critical product:
- Identified backup supplier
- Terms pre-negotiated
- Contact details current
- Tested at least annually
```

### Category Priorities
| Category | Criticality | Backup Required |
|----------|-------------|-----------------|
| Dairy | Critical | Yes - tested |
| Bread | Critical | Yes - tested |
| Meat | Critical | Yes - tested |
| Produce | High | Yes |
| Pantry | Medium | Identified |

### Keep Warm
```
Quarterly contact:
- Confirm still available
- Update pricing
- Check capacity
- Maintain relationship
```

### Annual Test
```
For critical categories:
- Place small test order
- Verify quality
- Check delivery
- Update records
```

## Activation Protocol

### When to Activate
```
Trigger backup when:
- Primary cannot deliver
- Quality issue with primary
- Primary at capacity
- Price dispute unresolved
- Supply disruption
```

### Activation Process
```
1. Confirm primary cannot supply
2. Contact backup immediately
3. Place order
4. Confirm delivery details
5. Notify receiving team
6. Update inventory system
7. Document activation
```

### Supply Risk Matrix
```
| Product | Risk Level | Mitigation |
|---------|------------|------------|
| Fresh milk | High | 2 backups, daily contact |
| Bread | High | 2 backups, early order |
```

### Risk Factors
```
Assess:
- Single source dependence
- Geographic concentration
- Seasonal availability
- Supplier stability
- Lead time requirements
```
