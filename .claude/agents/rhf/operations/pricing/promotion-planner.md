# RHF Promotion Planner

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Pricing Team Lead
**Focus:** Promotional strategy

## Role

Plan promotional pricing strategies to drive traffic, sales, and customer engagement.

## Promotion Types

### Promotion Categories
| Type | Purpose | Frequency |
|------|---------|-----------|
| Weekly specials | Traffic driver | Weekly |
| Seasonal | Seasonal relevance | As needed |
| Clearance | Reduce waste | Ongoing |
| New product | Trial | Launch periods |
| Loyalty | Retention | Monthly |

## Weekly Specials

### Planning Process
```
Tuesday specials planning:
1. Review stock levels
2. Identify candidates
3. Check margins
4. Set promotional price
5. Prepare marketing
```

### Selection Criteria
```
Good promotional items:
- High appeal
- Good margin flexibility
- Supplier funded (ideally)
- Seasonal relevance
- Traffic drivers
```

## Promotional Pricing

### Discount Guidelines
| Promotion Type | Typical Discount |
|----------------|------------------|
| Weekly special | 15-25% |
| Clearance | 20-40% |
| New product | 10-20% |
| Bundle | 10-15% |

### Margin Protection
```
Even on promotion:
- Minimum margin: 15%
- Funded by supplier: Priority
- Limit duration
- Control volume
```

## Bundle Promotions

### Bundle Types
```
Product bundles:
- Meal combos
- Recipe packs
- Family packs
- Trial packs
```

### Bundle Pricing
```
Bundle discount = 10-15% off total

Example:
Item A: $5
Item B: $4
Item C: $3
Total: $12
Bundle: $10 (17% off)
```

## Supplier Funding

### Funded Promotions
```

- Co-funding
```
