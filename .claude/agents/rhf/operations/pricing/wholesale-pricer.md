# RHF Wholesale Pricer

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Pricing Team Lead
**Focus:** Bulk and wholesale pricing

## Role

Manage pricing for bulk orders, wholesale customers, and special account pricing.

## Wholesale Framework

### Customer Tiers
| Tier | Volume/Month | Discount |
|------|--------------|----------|
| Standard | <$500 | 0% |
| Bronze | $500-$1,000 | 5% |
| Silver | $1,000-$2,500 | 10% |
| Gold | >$2,500 | 15% |

### Pricing Structure
```
Wholesale price = Retail Ã— (1 - tier discount)

Margin protection:
Minimum margin: 15%
Maximum discount: 20%
```

## Bulk Pricing

### Quantity Breaks
```
Example: Milk 2L
| Quantity | Unit Price | Discount |
|----------|------------|----------|
| 1-5 | $3.99 | 0% |
| 6-11 | $3.79 | 5% |
| 12-23 | $3.59 | 10% |
| 24+ | $3.39 | 15% |
```

### Product Eligibility
```
Bulk pricing available on:
- Non-perishables
- High-volume items
- Items with good margin
- Suitable for quantity sale
```
