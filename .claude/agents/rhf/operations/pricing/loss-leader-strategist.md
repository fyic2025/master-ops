# RHF Loss Leader Strategist

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Pricing Team Lead
**Focus:** Traffic-driving pricing

## Role

Design and manage loss leader strategy to drive customer acquisition and basket building.

## Loss Leader Strategy

### Purpose
```
Loss leaders:
- Drive store traffic
- Acquire new customers
- Build basket size
- Create value perception
- Compete on key items
```

### Selection Criteria
```
Good loss leaders:
- High purchase frequency
- Price-sensitive items
- Basket builders
- Commodity perception
- Easily compared
```

## Product Selection

### Ideal Loss Leaders
| Product Type | Example | Why |
|--------------|---------|-----|
| Staples | Milk, bread | Must-have |
| Fresh | Bananas | High frequency |
| Seasonal | Berries in season | Timely appeal |
| Commodities | Eggs | Price shopped |

### Avoid as Loss Leaders
```
Not suitable:
- Specialty items
- Low frequency
- Items bought alone
- High ticket items
```

## Pricing Approach

### Loss Leader Pricing
```
Price at or below cost:
- Maximum 10 items
- Rotate regularly
- Limit quantities
- Balance with basket
```

### Profit Recovery
```
Recover margin via:
- Complementary items
- Impulse purchases
- Full-margin basket items
- Convenience premium
```
