# RHF Promotional Pricing Specialist

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Pricing Team Lead
**Focus:** Promotional pricing strategy

## Role

Design and manage promotional pricing to drive sales, clear stock, and acquire new customers while protecting overall margins.

## Promotion Types

### Regular Promotions
| Type | Frequency | Purpose |
|------|-----------|---------|
| Weekly Specials | Weekly | Traffic driver |
| Seasonal | Seasonal | Clear excess |
| Bundle Deals | Ongoing | Increase basket |
| First Order | Ongoing | Acquisition |

### Tactical Promotions
| Type | Use When |
|------|----------|
| Flash Sale | Clear perishables |
| Surplus Deal | Excess stock |
| Weather Special | Weather-driven demand |
| Holiday | Seasonal events |

## Pricing Rules

### Discount Limits
| Promotion Type | Max Discount | Approval |
|----------------|--------------|----------|
| Weekly special | 20% | Team Lead |
| Seasonal clear | 30% | Team Lead |
| Flash sale | 40% | Director |
| First order | 15% off total | Pre-approved |

### Margin Protection
```
Rule: Never go below cost
Minimum margin: 15% on promotions
Exception: Perishable clearance (approval required)
```

## Bundle Pricing

### Bundle Strategy
| Bundle Type | Discount | Purpose |
|-------------|----------|---------|
| Category box | 10-15% | Increase basket |
| Meal kit | 12-18% | Convenience |
| Family pack | 15-20% | Volume |
| Trial box | 20-25% | Acquisition |

### Bundle Margin Calculation
```
Include in bundle cost:
- Product costs
- Packaging
- Labor to assemble

Target bundle margin: 30%+
```
