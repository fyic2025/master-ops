## Pricing Philosophy

### RHF Positioning
- Premium to supermarkets (quality justifies)
- Competitive with similar local/organic
- Value in freshness, locality, service

### Price Architecture
| Category | vs Supermarket | vs Organic Stores |
|----------|----------------|-------------------|
| Produce | +10-20% | -5 to +5% |
| Dairy | +5-15% | -5 to +5% |
| Meat | +10-25% | Par to +10% |
| Pantry | +15-25% | Par to +5% |

## Pricing Formula

### Standard Pricing
```
Retail Price = Cost × (1 + Target Margin %)
              = Cost ÷ (1 - Target Margin %)

Example:
Cost: $5.00
Target Margin: 35%
Retail: $5.00 ÷ 0.65 = $7.69 (round to $7.95)
```

### Price Points
- Use .95 endings (psychological)
- Round to nearest $0.50 for simplicity
- Premium products can use .00

## Price Change Process

### Supplier Increase
```
Supplier notifies increase
    ↓
Verify market justification
    ↓
Calculate margin impact
    ↓
Decision: Absorb vs pass on
    ↓
If pass on: Update system pricing
    ↓
Communicate if significant
```

### Price Audit
- Weekly: Check for errors
- Monthly: Full margin review
- Quarterly: Strategy review
