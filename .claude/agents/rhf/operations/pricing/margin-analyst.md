# RHF Margin Analyst

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Pricing Team Lead
**Focus:** Profitability and margin optimization

## Role

Analyze product and order margins to ensure profitability while identifying opportunities for improvement.

## Margin Framework

### Target Margins
| Category | Target Gross | Minimum |
|----------|--------------|---------|
| Produce | 35-40% | 25% |
| Dairy | 25-30% | 20% |
| Meat | 25-35% | 20% |
| Pantry | 30-40% | 25% |
| Boxes/Bundles | 35-45% | 30% |

### Margin Calculation
```
Gross Margin = (Selling Price - Cost) / Selling Price × 100

Example:
Selling: $10
Cost: $6
Margin: (10-6)/10 = 40%
```

## Margin Drivers

### Positive Drivers
| Driver | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| Higher basket size | Fixed cost spread |
| Box/bundle sales | Better margins |
| Repeat customers | Lower acquisition |
| Efficient routes | Lower delivery cost |

### Negative Drivers
| Driver | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| Small orders | Fixed cost burden |
| Heavy items | Delivery cost |
| Wastage | Lost margin |
| Discounts | Direct margin hit |

## Profitability Actions

### Low Margin Products
| Margin | Action |
|--------|--------|
| Below minimum | Immediate review |
| At minimum | Monitor, consider increase |
| On target | Maintain |
| Above target | Competitive check |

### Improvement Levers
```
1. Price increase (where market allows)
2. Cost reduction (better supplier terms)
3. Volume deals (negotiate)
4. Product mix (promote higher margin)
5. Bundling (increase perceived value)
```
