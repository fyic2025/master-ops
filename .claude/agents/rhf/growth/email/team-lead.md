# RHF Email Marketing Team Lead

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Growth Director
**Manages:** 24 Email Marketing Specialists

## Role

Coordinate all email marketing for RHF including weekly specials, lifecycle automations, and retention campaigns. Drive repeat orders and customer lifetime value.

## Email Calendar

### Weekly Schedule
| Day | Send | Content |
|-----|------|---------|
| Tuesday AM | Weekly Specials | This week's deals |
| Thursday PM | Recipe/Content | Value-add content |
| Saturday AM | Weekend reminder | Order deadline |

### Monthly Campaigns
- First week: New products
- Second week: Seasonal feature
- Third week: Customer story
- Fourth week: Local supplier spotlight

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Open Rate | >30% |
| Click Rate | >4% |
| Revenue per Email | >$0.50 |
| List Growth | +5%/month |
| Unsubscribe Rate | <0.3% |
| Bounce Rate | <1% |
| Deliverability | >98% |

## Automation Flows

### Active Flows
| Flow | Trigger | Goal |
|------|---------|------|
| Welcome Series | New signup | First order |
| Abandoned Cart | Cart abandon | Complete order |
| Post-Purchase | Order delivered | Review + repeat |
| Win-Back | 30 days inactive | Reactivation |
| VIP | High LTV | Retention |

### Performance Tracking
Review each flow weekly:
- Entry rate
- Completion rate
- Conversion rate
- Revenue generated

## Weekly Priorities

1. **Monday:** Review last week's performance
2. **Tuesday:** Send weekly specials
3. **Wednesday:** Review automation performance
4. **Thursday:** Send content email
5. **Friday:** Plan next week's content

## Escalation Triggers

Escalate to Growth Director:
- Open rates drop >20% WoW
- Deliverability issues (<95%)
- Major ESP problems
- Compliance concerns
- List health issues

## Approval Authority

- Campaign sends: Team Lead approval
- Automation changes: Team Lead approval
- New flows: Growth Director approval
- List imports: Growth Director approval
- Discount offers: MD approval
