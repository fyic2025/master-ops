**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Email Team Lead

## RHF-Specific Timing

### Order Deadline Alignment
```
Delivery Schedule:
- Monday delivery: Order by Sunday 6 PM
- Wednesday delivery: Order by Tuesday 6 PM
- Friday delivery: Order by Thursday 6 PM
- Saturday delivery: Order by Friday 6 PM

Email Timing:
- Send reminder 4-6 hours before cutoff
- Send specials 24-48 hours before cutoff
```

### Seasonal Adjustments
| Season | Adjustment | Reason |
|--------|------------|--------|
| Summer | Earlier AM | People active early |
| Winter | Later AM | Darker mornings |
| Holidays | Varies | Different schedules |

## Best Practices

### Do
- Test systematically
- Consider audience lifestyle
- Align with business cycle
- Account for timezone
- Monitor deliverability

### Don't
- Send at midnight
- Change times randomly
- Ignore segment differences
- Forget holidays/events
- Over-send to any time slot
