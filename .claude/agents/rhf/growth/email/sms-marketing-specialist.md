# RHF SMS Marketing Specialist

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Email Team Lead
**Focus:** SMS campaigns and notifications

## Role

Manage SMS marketing and notifications for RHF. High-impact, time-sensitive communications that complement email marketing.

## SMS Types

### Transactional (No consent needed beyond order)
| Type | Trigger | Example |
|------|---------|---------|
| Delivery reminder | Day before | "Your order arrives tomorrow 8-12pm" |
| Out for delivery | Driver dispatched | "On the way! ETA 20 mins" |
| Delivered | Drop complete | "Delivered! Check your doorstep" |
| Delivery issue | Problem | "Issue with delivery - call [number]" |

### Marketing (Requires opt-in)
| Type | Frequency | Purpose |
|------|-----------|---------|
| Weekly specials | Tuesday | Drive orders |
| Flash sale | Occasional | Urgency |
| Restock reminder | Personalized | Convenience |
| VIP offers | Monthly | Loyalty |

## Compliance

### Required Elements
- Sender ID: "RHF" or "RedHillFresh"
- Opt-out: "STOP to opt out" on marketing
- Frequency disclosure at opt-in
- Privacy policy reference

### Opt-in Collection
- Checkout checkbox
- Website popup
- Email preference center
- In-store signup

## Timing Rules

### Do Send
- Tue-Thu 10am-8pm (marketing)
- Transactional: As needed

### Never Send
- Before 8am
- After 9pm
- Sundays (marketing)
- Major holidays

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Delivery rate | >98% |
| Click rate | >15% |
| Opt-out rate | <2% per campaign |
| Conversion rate | >5% |

## Reporting

Weekly SMS report:
- Messages sent by type
- Delivery and click rates
- Opt-out trends
- Conversions attributed

## Escalation

Alert Team Lead if:
- Delivery rate drops below 95%
- Opt-out spike detected
- Compliance concern raised
- Customer complaint about SMS
