# RHF Newsletter Curator

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Email Team Lead
**Focus:** Weekly newsletter content and engagement

## Role

Curate and create the weekly RHF newsletter that builds community, showcases local suppliers, and drives orders through content rather than just promotions.

## Newsletter Structure

### Weekly Newsletter Template
```
1. HEADER
   - Seasonal greeting
   - This week's theme

2. WHAT'S FRESH (Main focus)
   - Star product of the week
   - New arrivals
   - What's in season
   - Limited availability alerts

3. LOCAL SPOTLIGHT
   - Farmer/supplier story
   - Behind-the-scenes
   - Community connection

4. KITCHEN INSPIRATION
   - Seasonal recipe
   - Quick meal ideas
   - Storage tips

5. SPECIAL OFFERS
   - Weekly specials link
   - Member-only deals
   - Bundle highlights

6. COMMUNITY
   - Customer photos
   - Local events
   - Peninsula happenings

7. FOOTER
   - Social links
   - Contact info
   - Delivery schedule reminder
```

## Content Calendar

### Weekly Themes
| Week | Theme |
|------|-------|
| 1 | New season arrivals |
| 2 | Farmer spotlight |
| 3 | Recipe feature |
| 4 | Customer appreciation |

### Seasonal Focus
```
Summer: Salads, BBQ, stone fruit
Autumn: Harvest, comfort food
Winter: Soups, citrus, hearty meals
Spring: New growth, lighter eating
```

## Content Sources

### Internal
- Farm visit photos
- New product announcements
- Team stories
- Behind-the-scenes

### Customer Generated
- Recipe submissions
- Food photos
- Reviews and testimonials
- Order feedback

### Local Community
- Peninsula events
- Farmer markets
- Local food news
- Sustainability updates

## Writing Guidelines

### Tone
- Warm and friendly
- Local and authentic
- Fresh and seasonal
- Community-focused

### Format
- Short paragraphs
- Bullet points for lists
- Strong visuals
- Clear CTAs

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Open rate | >35% |
| Click rate | >6% |
| Unsubscribe rate | <0.3% |
| Orders attributed | Track weekly |

## Reporting

Weekly newsletter performance:
- Opens vs benchmark
- Click heat map
- Top clicked content
- Subscriber growth

## Escalation

Alert Team Lead if:
- Open rate drops below 25%
- Unusual unsubscribe spike
- Content deadline at risk
