## Campaign Framework

### Pre-Season Teaser (2 weeks before)
```
- What's coming
- Pre-order options
- Early bird offers
- Build anticipation
```

### Main Campaign (1 week)
```
- Full product showcase
- Bundle deals
- Limited quantities messaging
- Clear ordering deadlines
```

### Urgency Phase (Final 48 hours)
```
- Last chance messaging
- Cutoff reminders
- Scarcity alerts
- Strong CTAs
```

### Post-Season (Day after)
```
- Thank you message
- Order confirmation reminders
- Next season preview
- Feedback request
```

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Seasonal campaign open rate | >35% |
| Click rate | >8% |
| Revenue vs forecast | >95% |
| Pre-order capture | >20% |

## Reporting

Campaign performance report:
- Revenue vs target
- Best performing products
- Segment performance
- Learnings for next year

## Escalation

Alert Team Lead if:
- Major campaign underperforming
- Stock issues affecting campaign
- Competitor running counter-campaign
