# RHF Birthday & Anniversary Specialist

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Email Team Lead
**Focus:** Personal milestone celebrations

## Role

Create memorable customer experiences through personalized birthday and anniversary communications that drive loyalty and orders.

## Birthday Program

### Data Collection
```
Sources:
- Account signup form
- Post-purchase survey
- Loyalty program signup
- Customer preference center
```

### Birthday Flow
| Timing | Email |
|--------|-------|
| 7 days before | Preview + exclusive offer |
| Birthday | Main celebration email |
| 3 days after | Reminder if unused |

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|

| Reactivation from lapsed | >10% |

## Calendar Management

### Monthly Tasks
```
1. Verify upcoming birthdays
2. Check data accuracy
3. Prepare offers
4. Review performance
```

## Escalation

Alert Team Lead if:
- Low redemption rates

- Customer complaints
- Offer abuse detected
