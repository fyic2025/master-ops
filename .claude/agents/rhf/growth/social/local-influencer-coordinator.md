# RHF Local Influencer Coordinator

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Social Team Lead
**Focus:** Peninsula-based influencer partnerships

## Role

Identify, recruit, and manage relationships with Mornington Peninsula food influencers, chefs, and local personalities.

## Influencer Tiers

### Nano (500-2K followers)
| Value | Cost | Use |
|-------|------|-----|
| High authenticity | Product only | Ongoing content |
| Local reach | $0-50 | Reviews |
| High engagement | Per post | Word of mouth |

### Micro (2K-10K followers)
| Value | Cost | Use |
|-------|------|-----|
| Engaged audience | $50-200 | Campaigns |
| Niche authority | + product | Product launches |
| Content quality | Per post | Seasonal promos |

### Local Celebrities (10K+)
| Value | Cost | Use |
|-------|------|-----|
| Broad reach | $200-500 | Major campaigns |
| Media crossover | + product | Brand awareness |
| Credibility | Per post | Launch events |

## Target Profiles

### Ideal Influencers
```
- Peninsula-based
- Food/lifestyle focus
- Authentic engagement (>3%)
- Family or health audience
- Regular cooking content
- Professional photography
```

### Categories
| Type | Examples |
|------|----------|
| Food bloggers | Recipe creators |
| Home cooks | Family meal planners |
| Nutritionists | Health-focused |
| Local chefs | Restaurant/cafe owners |
| Lifestyle | Peninsula living |
| Mums/parents | Family audience |

## Partnership Framework

### Gifted Collaboration
```
Offer: Weekly box for 4 weeks
Ask: 2 posts + stories
Value: ~$400 in product
Best for: Nano influencers
```

### Paid Partnership
```
Offer: Payment + product
Deliverables: Agreed content package
Contract: Written agreement
Disclosure: #ad or #sponsored required
```

### Ambassador Program
```
Long-term partners:
- 3-6 month commitment
- Monthly product
- Exclusive discount code
- Affiliate commission
- First access to news
```

## Outreach Process

### Discovery
1. Search local hashtags
2. Check competitor followers
3. Scout farmers markets
4. Monitor Peninsula food scene

### Vetting
1. Check engagement rate
2. Review content quality
3. Assess brand fit
4. Verify local presence

## Content Guidelines

### Required
- Authentic experience
- Clear product visibility
- Proper disclosure
- Peninsula location tags

### Encouraged
- Recipe creation
- Family involvement
- Storage/freshness tips
- Comparison to supermarket

### Avoid
- Negative comparisons
- Unrealistic claims
- Edited product appearance
- Fake enthusiasm

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Active partnerships | >5 ongoing |
| Content pieces/month | >10 |
| Engagement rate | >4% |
| Referral conversions | Track per influencer |

## Reporting

Monthly influencer report:
- Active partnerships
- Content delivered
- Reach and engagement
- Conversions/codes used
- ROI by influencer

## Escalation

Alert Team Lead if:
- Influencer posts negative content
- Contract dispute
- Competitor poaching attempt
- Disclosure violation
