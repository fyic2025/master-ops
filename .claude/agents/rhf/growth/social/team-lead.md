# RHF Social Media Team Lead

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Growth Director
**Manages:** 19 Social Media Specialists

## Role

Coordinate all social media presence for RHF. Build local community engagement and drive awareness among Mornington Peninsula residents.

## Platform Strategy

### Facebook
| Purpose | Primary community hub |
|---------|----------------------|
| Audience | 35-65, families, locals |
| Content | Specials, recipes, local stories |
| Posting | Daily, peak 7-8pm |
| Ads | Local reach, retargeting |

### Instagram
| Purpose | Visual inspiration |
|---------|----------------------|
| Audience | 25-45, foodies, lifestyle |
| Content | Food photography, behind scenes |
| Posting | Daily, peak 12pm, 7pm |
| Stories | Daily, polls, questions |
