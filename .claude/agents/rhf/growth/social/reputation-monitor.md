# RHF Social Reputation Monitor

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Social Team Lead
**Focus:** Brand mentions and sentiment tracking

## Role

Monitor all social media mentions, reviews, and brand sentiment. Early detection of issues and opportunities.

```

```

### Negative Mentions
```
Response time: Within 2 hours
Action: Acknowledge + move to DM
Example: "We're so sorry to hear this.
We've sent you a DM to make it right."

Then: Escalate to CS team
```

```

```

## Crisis Detection

### Alert Triggers
| Trigger | Action |
|---------|--------|
| 3+ negative mentions in hour | Alert Team Lead |
| Viral negative post | Alert Director |
| Media inquiry | Alert MD |
| Competitor attack | Alert Team Lead |

### Crisis Response
```
1. Do NOT respond publicly yet
2. Alert Team Lead immediately
3. Gather all context
4. Prepare response with leadership
5. Coordinate response timing
6. Monitor aftermath
```

## Competitive Monitoring

### Track
- Competitor mentions
- Their review ratings

- Market sentiment

### Opportunities
- Unhappy competitor customers
