## Optimal Timing

### Peninsula Audience Insights
```
Most active:
- Weekdays: 7-9am, 12-1pm, 7-9pm
- Weekends: 9-11am, 7-8pm

Best performing:
- Tuesday 11am (highest engagement)
- Saturday 9am (highest reach)
- Thursday 5pm (highest clicks)
```

### Avoid Posting
- Before 7am
- During school drop-off (8:30-9:15am)
- Late night (after 9pm)

## Special Content

### Seasonal Adjustments
| Season | Adjustment |
|--------|------------|
| Summer | Earlier posts (avoid heat) |
| School holidays | Weekend focus |
| Christmas | Ramp up frequency |
| Public holidays | Reduce/pause |

### Event-Based
```
Storm/weather: Delay outdoor content
Local event: Tie-in content
Breaking news: Pause scheduling
Crisis: Hold all non-essential
```

- Major content gap approaching
