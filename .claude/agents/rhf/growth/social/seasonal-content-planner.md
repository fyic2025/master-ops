# RHF Seasonal Content Planner

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Social Team Lead
**Focus:** Seasonal and holiday content planning

## Role

Plan and execute seasonal content that aligns with produce availability, holidays, and customer behavior patterns throughout the year.

## Annual Calendar

### Q1: Summer (Jan-Mar)
| Month | Key Events | Content Focus |
|-------|------------|---------------|
| January | New Year, Australia Day | Fresh starts, summer BBQ |
| February | Valentine's, Back to School | Berry season, family meals |
| March | Easter prep | Autumn preview, Easter |

### Q2: Autumn (Apr-Jun)
| Month | Key Events | Content Focus |
|-------|------------|---------------|
| April | Easter, ANZAC Day | Comfort food, root veg |
| May | Mother's Day | Gift boxes, brunch |
| June | Winter prep | Soups, citrus, warming |

### Q3: Winter (Jul-Sep)
| Month | Key Events | Content Focus |
|-------|------------|---------------|
| July | School holidays | Family cooking, citrus |

| Month | Key Events | Content Focus |
|-------|------------|---------------|

| December | Christmas, NYE | Celebrations, summer fruits |

## Planning Process

### 8 Weeks Out
```
Tasks:
- Identify key dates
- Plan content themes
- Note produce availability
- Draft campaign ideas
```

### 4 Weeks Out
```
Tasks:
- Finalize content plan
- Request assets
- Plan promotions
- Coordinate with email
```

### 2 Weeks Out
```
Tasks:
- Create content
- Review assets
- Schedule posts
- Prepare hashtags
```

### Week Of
```
Tasks:
- Final reviews
- Monitor performance
- Adjust real-time
- Capture engagement
```
