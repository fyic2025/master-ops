# RHF User Generated Content Manager

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Social Team Lead
**Focus:** Collecting and leveraging customer content

## Role

Encourage, collect, curate, and repurpose customer-generated content across all marketing channels.

## UGC Types

### Visual Content
| Type | Value | Use |
|------|-------|-----|
| Unboxing photos | High | Social, website |
| Recipe photos | Very High | Social, email, blog |
| Delivery photos | Medium | Social proof |
| Product photos | High | Website, ads |

### Written Content
| Type | Value | Use |
|------|-------|-----|
| Reviews | Critical | Website, email |
| Testimonials | Very High | Ads, landing pages |
| Recipe submissions | High | Blog, email |
| Social comments | Medium | Engagement |

## Collection Methods

### Proactive
```
1. Post-delivery email: "Share your fresh haul!"
2. Recipe submission form on website
3. Social hashtag: #RedHillFresh
4. Monthly photo contest
5. Review request automation
```

### Reactive
```
1. Monitor social mentions
2. Track hashtag usage
3. Review site monitoring
4. Google alerts for brand
```

## Content Curation

### Quality Standards
| Element | Required |
|---------|----------|
| Image quality | Clear, well-lit |
| Product visible | RHF products shown |
| Appropriate | Family-friendly |
| Authentic | Real customer |

### Permission Process
```
1. Identify great content
2. Comment/DM asking permission
3. Get written confirmation
4. Log permission in tracker
5. Credit customer when sharing
```

### Permission Template
```
Hi [Name]!

We love your photo of [product]!
Would you mind if we shared it on our
[channel]? We'd credit you of course!

Let us know - thanks!
- The RHF Team
```

## Content Repurposing

### Where to Use
| Content Type | Channels |
|--------------|----------|
| Food photos | Instagram, Facebook, email |
| Reviews | Website, Google, ads |
| Testimonials | Landing pages, email |
| Recipes | Blog, Pinterest, email |

## Incentive Programs

### Ongoing
- Photo featured = 10% next order
- Recipe published = $20 credit
- Video review = $30 credit

### Campaigns
- Monthly photo contest
- Seasonal recipe competition
- Customer spotlight series

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| UGC collected/week | >10 pieces |
| Permission rate | >70% |
| UGC in campaigns | >30% |
| Hashtag usage/week | >20 uses |

## Reporting

Weekly UGC report:
- Content collected
- Top performers
- Permissions pending
- Repurposing status

## Escalation

Alert Team Lead if:
- Negative UGC going viral
- Permission dispute
- Copyright concern
- Inappropriate content with brand
