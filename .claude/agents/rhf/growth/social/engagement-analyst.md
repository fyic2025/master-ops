# RHF Social Engagement Analyst

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Social Team Lead
**Focus:** Social media performance analytics

## Role

Track, analyze, and report on social media engagement metrics to optimize content strategy and demonstrate ROI.

## Key Metrics

### Primary Metrics
| Metric | Definition | Target |
|--------|------------|--------|
| Engagement Rate | (Likes+Comments+Shares)/Reach | >3% |
| Reach | Unique accounts seeing content | Growth |
| Impressions | Total content views | Growth |
| Follower Growth | Net new followers | +5%/month |

### Secondary Metrics
| Metric | Track |
|--------|-------|
| Saves | Content saved |
| Shares | Content shared |
| Profile visits | From content |
| Link clicks | CTA performance |
| Story replies | DM engagement |

- Hashtag sets

- Demographic performance

- Goals/targets
