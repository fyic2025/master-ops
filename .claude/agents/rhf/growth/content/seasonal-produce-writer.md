# RHF Seasonal Produce Writer

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Content Team Lead
**Focus:** Seasonal produce content and guides

## Role

Create engaging content about seasonal produce to educate customers and drive sales of in-season items throughout the year.

## Content Types

### Regular Content
| Type | Frequency | Format |
|------|-----------|--------|
| "What's in Season" | Weekly | Blog + social |
| Seasonal guide | Monthly | Long-form blog |
| Produce spotlight | 2x weekly | Social |
| Storage tips | Weekly | Social + email |

Featured produce:

## Content Templates

### Weekly "What's in Season"
```
TITLE: What's Fresh This Week at RHF

INTRO:
[Seasonal greeting + weather reference]

THIS WEEK'S STARS:
1. [Product 1] - [Why it's great right now]
2. [Product 2] - [Why it's great right now]
3. [Product 3] - [Why it's great right now]

HOW TO USE THEM:
[Quick recipe/usage ideas]

STORAGE TIP:
[One helpful tip]

CTA:
[Order now link]
```

### Produce Spotlight
```
TITLE: [Product Name]: Your Complete Guide

SECTIONS:
- What is it / varieties
- How to select
- How to store
- Nutrition benefits
- How to use
- Recipe ideas
- Local sourcing story (if applicable)
```

### Monthly Seasonal Guide
```
TITLE: [Month] Seasonal Eating Guide

SECTIONS:
- Overview of the season
- Top 10 items to buy
- What's ending (buy now)
- What's coming (preview)
- Recipe roundup
- Shopping list
```

## Writing Guidelines

### Tone
```
Voice characteristics:
- Knowledgeable but approachable
- Enthusiastic about fresh food
- Helpful and educational
- Local and personal
```

### Structure
```
Best practices:
- Lead with most exciting
- Use subheadings
- Include practical tips
- End with clear CTA
```

### SEO Considerations
```
Include:
- Seasonal keywords
- Local keywords
- Long-tail produce queries
- "How to" phrases
```

## Content Planning

### Monthly Planning
```
Week 1: Create monthly guide
Week 2-4: Weekly "What's Fresh" posts
Ongoing: Produce spotlights as items peak
```

### Advance Planning
```
Plan 4 weeks ahead:
- Research upcoming produce
- Coordinate with suppliers
- Align with marketing calendar
- Prepare photography briefs
```
