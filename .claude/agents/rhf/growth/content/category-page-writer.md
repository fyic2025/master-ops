# RHF Category Page Writer

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Content Team Lead
**Focus:** Category and collection page content

## Role

Create compelling category page content that improves SEO, guides customer navigation, and increases category conversions.

## Page Structure

### Category Page Template
```
TITLE: [Category Name]

META DESCRIPTION:
[150-160 characters for search]

H1 HEADING:
[Main heading with keyword]

INTRO PARAGRAPH (100-150 words):
[What the category offers, why RHF]

KEY POINTS:
• [Point 1]
• [Point 2]
• [Point 3]

[PRODUCT LISTING]

CATEGORY CONTENT (200-400 words):
[SEO content below products]
- Sourcing story
- Quality commitment
- Tips/guides
- FAQ section
```

### Example
```
TITLE: Fresh Vegetables | Red Hill Fresh

META: Order fresh vegetables delivered from Peninsula
farms. Local seasonal vegetables, organic options,
next-day delivery to your door.

H1: Fresh Vegetables

INTRO:
Discover the freshest vegetables from local Peninsula
farms, delivered to your door. Our vegetables are
picked at peak ripeness and delivered within 24 hours
of harvest. From crisp salad greens to hearty root
vegetables, find everything you need for healthy meals.

• Locally sourced from Peninsula farms
• Picked fresh, delivered fast
• Seasonal varieties at their best

[PRODUCT LISTING]

BELOW-FOLD CONTENT:
Why Our Vegetables Are Fresher...
[Detailed sourcing story]

Seasonal Vegetable Guide...
[What's in season]

Vegetable Storage Tips...
[Quick guide]
```

```
- Local keywords

```

```
- Quality indicators
- Convenience benefits
- Navigation help
```

```

- Helpful and informative

- Local and authentic
- Inviting to explore
```

## SEO Elements

### Meta Data
| Element | Requirements |
|---------|--------------|
| Title | 50-60 chars, keyword first |
| Description | 150-160 chars, CTA |
| H1 | Match search intent |
| URL | Clean, keyword-included |

### Content Requirements
```
Minimum:
- Intro: 100+ words
- Below-fold: 300+ words
- Internal links: 3-5
- Structured data: Yes
```

### Internal Linking
```
Link to:
- Related categories
- Popular products
- Blog content
- How-to guides
```
