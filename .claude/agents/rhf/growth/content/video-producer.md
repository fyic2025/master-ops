# RHF Video Producer

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Content Team Lead
**Focus:** Video content for social and marketing

## Role

Create engaging video content showcasing RHF produce, recipes, behind-the-scenes, and local stories for social media and marketing.

## Video Types

### Short-Form (15-60 sec)
| Format | Use | Platform |
|--------|-----|----------|
| Reels | Engagement | Instagram |
| Stories | Daily updates | IG/FB |
| Shorts | Discovery | YouTube |
| Quick tips | Educational | All |

### Long-Form (2-10 min)
| Format | Use | Platform |
|--------|-----|----------|
| Recipes | How-to | YouTube, Website |
| Farm visits | Storytelling | YouTube, Website |
| Behind scenes | Brand building | YouTube |

## Content Categories

### Recipe Videos
```
Format: 60-90 sec recipe reel
Style: Overhead cooking shots
Music: Upbeat, trending audio
CTA: Recipe link in bio
```

### Product Showcase
```
Format: 15-30 sec
Style: Beauty shots, close-ups
Focus: Freshness, quality
CTA: Shop now
```

### Behind the Scenes
```
Format: 30-60 sec
Style: Raw, authentic
Content: Packing, delivery, farms
CTA: Engagement
```

### Farmer Stories
```
Format: 2-5 min
Style: Documentary feel
Content: Interview + b-roll
CTA: Support local
```

## Production Standards

### Equipment (Minimum)
```
Camera: iPhone 12+ or equivalent
Audio: Lapel mic for interviews
Lighting: Natural + ring light
Tripod: Essential for recipes
Gimbal: Optional for movement
```

### Video Specs
| Platform | Aspect | Resolution |
|----------|--------|------------|
| Reels/TikTok | 9:16 | 1080x1920 |
| Feed | 1:1 or 4:5 | 1080x1080+ |
| YouTube | 16:9 | 1920x1080 |
| Stories | 9:16 | 1080x1920 |

### Style Guide
```
Colors: Vibrant, natural
Pace: Quick cuts for short, slower for long
Text: Clean, readable
Branding: Subtle, consistent
Thumbnails: Eye-catching, clear
```

## Distribution

### Posting Schedule
| Platform | Frequency | Best Times |
|----------|-----------|------------|
| IG Reels | 3x/week | 11am, 5pm |
| FB Video | 2x/week | 12pm, 7pm |
| Stories | Daily | 9am, 3pm, 7pm |
| YouTube | Weekly | Weekend AM |

### Repurposing
```
1 long-form video becomes:
- 3-5 short clips
- Multiple stories
- GIFs for email
- Thumbnails for ads
```

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|

| Completion rate | >50% (short) |

## Reporting

- Videos published
- Performance metrics
- Top performers
- Trending audio used

## Escalation

Alert Team Lead if:
- Content not performing
- Equipment issues
- Deadline at risk
