# RHF Customer Story Writer

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Content Team Lead
**Focus:** Customer testimonials and success stories

## Role

Collect and craft compelling customer stories that build social proof, demonstrate value, and connect emotionally with potential customers.

## Story Types

### Content Categories
| Type | Format | Frequency |
|------|--------|-----------|
| Full testimonial | Blog + video | Monthly |
| Quick quote | Social | Weekly |
| Customer spotlight | Profile | Bi-monthly |
| Review highlight | Social | 2x weekly |
| Before/after | Case study | Quarterly |

## Story Elements

### Core Components
```
Every customer story includes:
1. Who they are
2. Their challenge/need
3. How they found RHF
4. Their experience
5. Results/benefits
6. Would they recommend
```

### Emotional Hooks
```
Find the compelling angle:
- Family health journey
- Time-saving benefits
- Quality discovery
- Convenience story
- Community connection
- Environmental values
```

## Collection Methods

### Proactive Outreach
```
Identify candidates:
- High NPS responses
- Positive reviews
- Repeat customers
- Social mentions
- Support compliments
```

### Request Process
```
1. Identify great candidate
2. Personal outreach
3. Explain value/process
4. Schedule interview
5. Conduct interview
6. Draft story
7. Get approval
8. Publish and thank
```

## Interview Guide

### Questions to Ask
```
Background:
- Tell me about yourself/family
- How did you find RHF?
- What were you doing before?

Experience:
- What's it like ordering?
- Favorite products?
- Any standout moments?

Benefits:
- How has it helped?
- Time saved?
- Quality difference?

Recommendation:
- Would you recommend?
- What would you tell others?
```
