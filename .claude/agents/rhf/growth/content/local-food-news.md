# RHF Local Food News Writer

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Content Team Lead
**Focus:** Local food industry news and trends

## Role

Cover local food news, Peninsula food scene updates, and industry trends to position RHF as the go-to source for local food information.

## Content Types

### News Categories
| Category | Examples | Frequency |
|----------|----------|-----------|
| Local events | Markets, festivals | Weekly |
| Industry news | Regulations, trends | As needed |
| Producer news | Farm updates, openings | Monthly |
| Food trends | What's hot | Monthly |
| Community | Local food initiatives | Bi-monthly |

## Writing Guidelines

### News Style
```
Requirements:
- Lead with key info
- Answer who/what/when/where
- Quote sources
- Be timely
- Stay factual
```

### Tone
```
Voice:
- Informative
- Enthusiastic about local
- Community-focused
- Not promotional (unless warranted)
```

### Local Expertise
```
Demonstrate:
- Peninsula knowledge
- Industry understanding
- Community connection
- Insider perspective
```
