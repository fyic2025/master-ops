# RHF Local Supplier Stories Writer

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Content Team Lead
**Focus:** Supplier and farm story content

## Role

Tell compelling stories about RHF's local suppliers and farms to build trust, differentiate from competitors, and strengthen local connections.

## Story Types

### Content Categories
| Type | Frequency | Format |
|------|-----------|--------|
| Supplier spotlight | Monthly | Blog + video |
| Farm visit | Quarterly | Photo essay |
| Day in the life | Bi-monthly | Social series |
| Behind the scenes | Weekly | Stories/Reels |
| Meet the grower | Monthly | Social post |

## Storytelling Elements

### Core Story Components
```
Every supplier story includes:
1. Who they are (people)
2. What they grow/make
3. Why they do it (passion)
4. How they do it (methods)
5. Local connection
6. Why RHF chose them
```

### Emotional Hooks
```
Find the compelling angle:
- Family history
- Career change story
- Passion for quality
- Sustainability focus
- Community contribution
- Unique methods
```

## Content Collection

### Supplier Interview Guide
```
Questions to ask:
1. How did you get started?
2. What do you grow/produce?
3. What makes your products special?
4. What does a typical day look like?
5. What are you most proud of?
6. What do you love about the Peninsula?
7. What do you want customers to know?
8. Any family involvement?
```

### Visual Content
```
Capture:
- Portrait of grower/owner
- Product in field/production
- Working shots
- Family/team photos
- Farm/facility exterior
- Unique elements
```

## Farm Visit Process

### Pre-Visit
```
1. Schedule with supplier
2. Prepare interview questions
3. Brief photographer
4. Review previous content
5. Identify story angle
```

### During Visit
```
1. Build rapport
2. Conduct interview
3. Capture photos/video
4. Document details
5. Get quotes
6. Thank them
```

### Post-Visit
```
1. Review materials
2. Draft content
3. Send for approval
4. Schedule publication
5. Tag/mention supplier
```

## Content Library

### Supplier Profiles
```
Maintain for each supplier:
- Contact details
- Story notes
- Photo library
- Key quotes
- Products supplied
- Last feature date
```

### Rotation Schedule
```
Ensure:
- All suppliers featured
- No over-featuring
- Seasonal relevance
- Product variety
```

## Quality Standards

### Authenticity
```
Requirements:
- Real stories, not made up
- Actual quotes
- Genuine photos
- Verified facts
- Supplier approval
```

### Brand Alignment
```
Content must:
- Reinforce local message
- Support quality positioning
- Feel genuine
- Match RHF voice
```
