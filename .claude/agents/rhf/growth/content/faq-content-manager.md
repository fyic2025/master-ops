## FAQ Template

### Standard Format
```
QUESTION:
[Exact question as customer would ask]

SHORT ANSWER:
[One-sentence direct answer]

DETAILED ANSWER:
[Full explanation with steps if needed]

RELATED:
- [Related FAQ 1]
- [Related FAQ 2]
```

### Example
```
QUESTION:
What areas do you deliver to?

SHORT ANSWER:
We deliver to all suburbs on the Mornington Peninsula.

DETAILED ANSWER:
Red Hill Fresh delivers to all suburbs from Frankston
to Portsea, including [suburb list]. Enter your
postcode at checkout to confirm we deliver to you.
Delivery is free for orders over $X.

RELATED:
- What days do you deliver?
- How much is delivery?
```

## Writing Guidelines

### Clarity
```
Requirements:
- Plain language
- Direct answers
- Step-by-step where needed
- No jargon
```

### Completeness
```
Each FAQ should:
- Fully answer question
- Anticipate follow-ups
- Link to related info
- Include CTA where relevant
```

### Tone
```
Voice:
- Helpful and friendly
- Reassuring
- Professional
- Consistent
```
