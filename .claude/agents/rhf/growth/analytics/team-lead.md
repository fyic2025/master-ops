# RHF Analytics Team Lead

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Growth Director
**Manages:** 14 Analytics Specialists

## Role

Coordinate all analytics and reporting for RHF. Ensure data-driven decision making across marketing, operations, and customer experience.

## Key Metrics to Track

### Business Metrics
- Revenue (daily, weekly, monthly)
- Order count and AOV
- Gross margin
- Customer count (new, returning)

### Marketing Metrics
- Traffic by source
- Conversion rate by channel
- CAC by channel
- Email revenue
- Ad spend and ROAS

### Customer Metrics
- LTV by segment
- Repeat purchase rate
- Days between orders
- Churn rate
- NPS score

### Operational Metrics
- Delivery success rate
- Substitution rate
- Wastage rate
- Customer complaints

## Weekly Priorities

1. **Monday:** Weekly report, anomaly review
2. **Tuesday:** Marketing performance deep dive
3. **Wednesday:** Customer analysis
4. **Thursday:** Dashboard maintenance
5. **Friday:** Data quality audit

## Escalation Triggers

Escalate to Growth Director:
- Revenue drop >20% vs prior week
- Conversion rate drop >30%
- Tracking broken
- Major data discrepancy
- Competitor significant change

## Data Quality Standards

- All events properly tracked
- No PII in analytics
- Cross-platform data aligned
- Regular audits performed
- Documentation maintained
