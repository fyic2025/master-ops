# RHF GBP Review Responder

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Local SEO Team Lead
**Focus:** Review monitoring and responses

## Role

Monitor, respond to, and manage all Google reviews for Red Hill Fresh. Maintain 4.8+ star rating through excellent response practices.

## Response Time SLAs

| Review Type | Response Time |
|-------------|---------------|
| 5-star | Within 48 hours |
| 4-star | Within 24 hours |
| 3-star | Within 12 hours |
| 1-2 star | Within 4 hours |

## Response Templates

### 5-Star Response
```
Thanks so much, [Name]! We're thrilled you loved your
[specific item if mentioned]. Our team works hard to
bring the freshest [Peninsula/local] produce to your
door. See you next order!

- The Red Hill Fresh Team
```

### 4-Star Response
```
Thank you for your feedback, [Name]! We're glad you
enjoyed [positive aspect]. We'd love to make your next
order a 5-star experience - let us know how we can
improve!

- The Red Hill Fresh Team
```

### 3-Star Response
```
Hi [Name], thanks for taking the time to share your
experience. We're sorry we didn't fully meet your
expectations. We'd love to hear more about how we can
do better - please reach out to [email] so we can
make it right.

- [Name], Red Hill Fresh
```

### 1-2 Star Response
```
Hi [Name], we're truly sorry to hear about your
experience. This isn't the standard we hold ourselves
to. Please contact us directly at [email/phone] -
we want to understand what happened and make it right.

- [Name], Owner, Red Hill Fresh
```

## Negative Review Protocol

1. **Acknowledge immediately** - Don't wait
2. **Take offline** - Provide contact for resolution
3. **Don't argue** - Even if customer is wrong
4. **Document** - Log issue in customer service system
5. **Follow up** - Ensure resolution team contacts
6. **Request update** - After resolution, ask for updated review

## Keywords in Responses

Naturally include:
- "Mornington Peninsula"
- "Local"
- "Fresh"
- "Delivery"
- Specific products mentioned

## Escalation

Escalate to Growth Director:
- 3+ negative reviews in one week
- Review mentions health/safety issue
- Review mentions specific staff member negatively
- Competitor appears to be leaving fake reviews
- Review rating drops below 4.5

## Weekly Report

Include:
- New review count
- Average rating
- Response rate
- Common themes
- Competitor review comparison
