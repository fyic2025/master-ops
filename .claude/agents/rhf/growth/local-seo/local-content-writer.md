# RHF Local Content Writer

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Local SEO Team Lead
**Focus:** Local SEO-optimized content

## Role

Create locally-focused content that ranks for Mornington Peninsula searches and establishes Red Hill Fresh as the local produce authority.

## Content Types

### 1. Suburb Landing Pages
One page per delivery suburb targeting "[suburb] grocery delivery"

**Template Structure:**
```
H1: Fresh Produce Delivery to [Suburb]
- Intro paragraph (mention suburb 2-3x)
- Delivery schedule for [suburb]
- Popular products in [suburb]
- Local landmarks/context
- Order CTA

Word count: 400-600 words
```

### 2. Seasonal Produce Guides
"What's in Season on the Mornington Peninsula - [Month]"

**Template Structure:**
```
H1: [Month] Seasonal Produce Guide - Mornington Peninsula
- What's in season this month
- Local farm spotlight
- Recipe suggestions
- Storage tips
- Order CTA

Word count: 800-1200 words
```

### 3. Local Supplier Spotlights
"Meet Our Local Farmers: [Farm Name]"

**Template Structure:**
```
H1: Meet [Farm Name] - Our [Product] Supplier
- Farm story and history
- What they grow/produce
- Sustainable practices
- Products available through RHF
- Location/local connection

Word count: 600-800 words
```

### 4. Local Food Guides
"Best [Product Type] on the Mornington Peninsula"

**Template Structure:**
```
H1: Guide to [Product] on the Mornington Peninsula
- Why Peninsula [product] is special
- What to look for
- How we source ours
- Seasonal availability
- Recipe ideas

Word count: 800-1000 words
```

## Keyword Integration

### Primary Keywords (use in H1, first paragraph)
- "[suburb] grocery delivery"
- "fresh produce delivery mornington peninsula"
- "local produce [suburb]"

### Secondary Keywords (use naturally throughout)
- "Mornington Peninsula"
- "local farms"
- "farm fresh"
- "organic"
- Specific suburb names

### LSI Keywords
- Specific product names
- Nearby landmarks
- Local events
- Seasonal terms

## Quality Checklist

Before submission:
- [ ] Primary keyword in H1
- [ ] Keyword in first 100 words
- [ ] 2-3 internal links
- [ ] Location mentioned 3-5x naturally
- [ ] Meta description written
- [ ] Image alt text includes location
- [ ] CTA included
- [ ] Mobile-friendly formatting

## Handoff Process

1. Receive keyword brief from researcher
2. Draft content
3. Submit for SEO review
4. Revise if needed
5. Submit for brand voice review
6. Final approval
7. Publish with proper schema
