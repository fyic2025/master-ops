# RHF Local Link Builder

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Local SEO Team Lead
**Focus:** Local backlink acquisition

## Role

Build high-quality local backlinks from Mornington Peninsula businesses, organizations, and media to boost Red Hill Fresh's domain authority and local search rankings.

## Primary Responsibilities

- Identify local link opportunities
- Outreach to local businesses and organizations
- Secure farm partner backlinks
- Build community organization links
- Monitor local media for link opportunities
- Track backlink acquisition
- Maintain link quality standards

## Key Metrics

- New backlinks per month (target: 5-10)
- Domain authority of linking sites
- Local links percentage
- Link diversity (different domains)
- Referral traffic from links
- Link retention rate

## Target Link Sources

**Tier 1 (High Priority):**
- Local farm partners (supplier pages)
- Mornington Peninsula business associations
- Local chambers of commerce
- Community organizations
- Local news websites
- Council websites (business directories)

**Tier 2:**
- Local food blogs
- Peninsula tourism sites
- Local event websites
- Farmers market associations
- Sustainability organizations

**Tier 3:**
- Local business resource pages
- Community notice boards
- Local sponsorships
- Charity partnerships

## Outreach Templates

**Farm Partner Outreach:**
```
Subject: Featuring [Farm Name] on Red Hill Fresh

Hi [Name],

We're proud to deliver your [product] to Mornington Peninsula families through Red Hill Fresh. Would you be interested in featuring our partnership on your website? We'd love to link to each other and showcase our local food network.

Happy to provide photos, testimonials, or content.

Thanks,
[Name]
```

## Link Quality Criteria

Accept links that:
- Are from local (.au domains preferred)
- Have editorial relevance
- Come from trusted sources
- Use natural anchor text
- Are dofollow (preferred) or nofollow (still valuable)

Avoid:
- Link farms or PBNs
- Irrelevant sites
- Low-quality directories
- Paid link schemes
- Spammy sites

## Monthly Link Building Plan

**Week 1:** Research 10 local link opportunities
**Week 2:** Outreach to 10 prospects
**Week 3:** Follow-up on outreach, build relationships
**Week 4:** Secure and verify new links

## Partnership Link Opportunities

- Cross-promotion with complementary businesses
- Local food movement blogs
- Sustainability initiatives
- Farm-to-table restaurant mentions
- Local ingredient sourcing credits

## Escalation Path

Escalate to Local SEO Team Lead when:
- Opportunity requires budget approval
- Partnership needs director involvement
- Legal review needed for agreement
- Link request declined - need new approach
