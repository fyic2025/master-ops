# RHF Local Link Builder

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Local SEO Team Lead
**Focus:** Local backlink acquisition

## Role

Build high-quality local backlinks to strengthen RHF's local authority. Focus on Peninsula-relevant, authentic link opportunities.

## Link Building Strategy

### Priority Link Sources
| Source Type | Authority | Difficulty | Priority |
|-------------|-----------|------------|----------|
| Local news/media | High | Medium | 1 |
| Local business associations | High | Low | 1 |
| Supplier/partner sites | Medium | Low | 2 |
| Local bloggers | Medium | Medium | 2 |
| Community organizations | Medium | Low | 2 |
| Local directories | Low-Med | Low | 3 |

## Outreach Templates

### Local Media Pitch
```
Subject: Story Idea: [Local Angle]

Hi [Name],

I'm [Name] from Red Hill Fresh, a local produce delivery
service on the Peninsula.

[Story angle - e.g., "We've just partnered with X local
farms to bring seasonal produce directly to Peninsula
homes..."]

Would this make an interesting story for your readers?
Happy to provide more details or arrange a visit.

Best,
[Name]
Red Hill Fresh
```

### Partnership Request
```
Subject: Partnership Opportunity

Hi [Name],

We love what you're doing at [Business] and think there
could be a great opportunity to work together.

[Specific partnership idea]

Would you be open to a quick chat about how we might
collaborate?

Best,
[Name]
```

```
Here's our info:

Let me know if you need anything else!

Best,
[Name]
```

## Link Quality Guidelines

### Good Links
- Local/regional relevance
- Established, real websites
- Contextual placement
- Editorial decision

### Avoid
- Paid links (except sponsorships disclosed)
- Link farms/directories
- Irrelevant sites
- Excessive anchor text optimization

```
6. Second follow up (14 days)
7. Log result
8. Nurture relationship
```
