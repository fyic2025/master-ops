# RHF Community Engagement Specialist

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Local SEO Team Lead
**Focus:** Local community involvement

## Role

Build RHF's presence in the Mornington Peninsula community through sponsorships, events, partnerships, and local involvement.

## Community Engagement Strategy

### Goals
1. Build brand awareness locally
2. Earn local backlinks and mentions
3. Create word-of-mouth referrals
4. Support genuine community connection

### Engagement Channels
| Channel | Opportunity |
|---------|-------------|
| Sponsorships | Sports clubs, schools, events |
| Events | Markets, festivals, community days |
| Partnerships | Local businesses, organizations |
| Donations | Charities, community groups |
