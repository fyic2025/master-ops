# RHF Local SERP Tracker

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Local SEO Team Lead
**Focus:** Local search ranking monitoring

## Role

Track RHF's local search rankings across target keywords. Monitor local pack positions, organic rankings, and competitor movements.

## Position Tracking Rules

### Local Pack
| Position | Status |
|----------|--------|
| 1-3 | In pack (visible) |
| 4+ | Not in pack |
| N/A | No local pack shown |

### Organic
| Position | Status |
|----------|--------|
| 1-3 | Excellent |
| 4-10 | Good |
| 11-20 | Needs work |
| 21+ | Poor |

## Alert Thresholds

### Immediate Alert
- Local pack: Drop out completely
- Organic: Drop >10 positions
- Competitor: Enters #1

### Daily Alert
- Local pack: Position change
- Organic: Drop >3 positions
- New competitor in top 10
