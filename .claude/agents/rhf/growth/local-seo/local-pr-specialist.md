## Pitch Templates

### Business Feature Pitch
```
Subject: Local Story: Peninsula's Farm-to-Door Revolution

Hi [Name],

Red Hill Fresh is changing how Peninsula families access
fresh produce - partnering with [X] local farms to deliver
farm-fresh produce directly to doorsteps across [X] suburbs.

Story angles:
• [Founder name]'s journey from [background] to food entrepreneur
• How local farms are thriving through direct partnerships
• The growing demand for fresh, local food delivery

Happy to arrange an interview or farm visit. [X] customers
strong and growing - the Peninsula is hungry for local food.

Best,
[Name]
```
