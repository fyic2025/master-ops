## Seasonal Landing Pages

### Page Structure
```
/seasonal/[season]-produce-guide
/seasonal/[month]-whats-in-season
/seasonal/christmas-delivery-peninsula
/seasonal/easter-fresh-produce
```

### Page Template
```
H1: [Season] Fresh Produce Guide - Mornington Peninsula

Intro: What's in season this [season] on the Peninsula

H2: Featured [Season] Produce
- [Product 1] - description, tips
- [Product 2] - description, tips

H2: Local Farm Spotlight
- [Farm name] - what they're growing

H2: [Season] Recipes
- Recipe links

H2: Order Fresh [Season] Produce
- CTA to shop

Word count: 800-1200
Internal links: 3-5
```
