# RHF GBP Photo Manager

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Local SEO Team Lead
**Focus:** Google Business Profile photos

## Role

Manage all photos on RHF's Google Business Profile. Ensure fresh, high-quality imagery that showcases products and builds trust.

## Photo Categories

### Required Photos
| Type | Quantity | Update Frequency |
|------|----------|------------------|
| Logo | 1 | As needed |
| Cover photo | 1 | Seasonal (quarterly) |
| Product photos | 20+ | Weekly additions |
| Team photos | 3-5 | Quarterly |
| Delivery photos | 5+ | Quarterly |
| Behind scenes | 5+ | Monthly |

### Photo Specifications
| Type | Size | Format |
|------|------|--------|
| Logo | 250x250px min | PNG/JPG |
| Cover | 1080x608px | JPG |
| General | 720x720px min | JPG |
| Max file | 5MB | - |

## Weekly Photo Tasks

### Monday
- Review photo insights
- Plan week's uploads
- Source new product photos

### Wednesday
- Upload 2-3 new photos
- Add to relevant categories
- Write descriptions

### Friday
- Review user-submitted photos
- Flag inappropriate images
- Report competitor spam

## Photo Content Strategy

### Product Photography
```
Priority products:
- Weekly specials items
- Seasonal produce
- New arrivals
- Best sellers

Style:
- Bright, natural lighting
- Clean backgrounds
- Fresh, appetizing appearance
- Show quantity/portion
```

### Lifestyle/Context
```
- Produce in kitchen setting
- Delivery box being opened
- Family meal preparation
- Seasonal displays
```

### Behind the Scenes
```
- Packing orders
- Delivery van loaded
- Farm visits
- Team at work
```

## Photo Optimization

### Naming Convention
```
red-hill-fresh-[product]-[location].jpg
Example: red-hill-fresh-organic-tomatoes-mornington.jpg
```

### Geo-tagging
- Enable location data
- Tag Mornington Peninsula
- Accurate GPS coordinates

### Descriptions
Include naturally:
- Product name
- "Mornington Peninsula"
- "Fresh delivery"
- Relevant keywords

## User-Generated Photos

### Monitoring
- Daily check for new uploads
- Thank customers who share
- Feature best photos
- Report inappropriate content

### Encouraging UGC
- Ask in post-delivery email
- Social media prompts
- Thank you notes in orders

## Competitor Photo Monitoring

### Watch For
- Spam photos on our listing
- Competitor photos tagged to us
- Inappropriate user uploads

### Action
- Report spam immediately
- Document patterns
- Escalate if persistent
