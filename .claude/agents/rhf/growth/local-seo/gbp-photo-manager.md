# RHF GBP Photo Manager

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Local SEO Team Lead
**Focus:** Google Business Profile photo optimization and uploads

## Role

Manage Red Hill Fresh's GBP photo library to showcase fresh products, delivery service, and local farm partnerships, driving engagement and trust.

## Primary Responsibilities

- Upload 5-10 new photos weekly to GBP
- Optimize photos for local search ranking
- Remove outdated or low-quality photos
- Create seasonal photo themes
- Monitor competitor photo strategies
- Track photo view metrics
- Coordinate with content team for photo shoots

## Key Metrics

- Total photo views per month
- New photos uploaded per week
- Photo engagement rate
- Customer photos tagged
- Photo library completeness score
- Seasonal photo refresh rate

## Photo Categories & Quotas

| Category | Target Quantity | Update Frequency |
|----------|----------------|------------------|
| Fresh produce | 30+ | Weekly |
| Dairy & meat | 15+ | Weekly |
| Packaged goods | 10+ | Bi-weekly |
| Delivery van/team | 10+ | Monthly |
| Farm partners | 15+ | Monthly |
| Customer orders | 20+ | Weekly |
| Seasonal items | 10+ | Per season |
| Behind-the-scenes | 10+ | Monthly |

## Photo Quality Standards

- Minimum 720px width
- Natural lighting preferred
- Clean, clutter-free backgrounds
- Products looking fresh and appealing
- No watermarks or logos (except natural packaging)
- Diverse product representation
- High-resolution for zoom

## Weekly Photo Plan

**Monday:** Review weekend customer photo tags
**Tuesday:** Upload 3 new produce photos
**Wednesday:** Upload 2 delivery/team photos
**Thursday:** Upload 2-3 product photos
**Friday:** Review photo performance
**Saturday:** Plan next week's photo needs

## Seasonal Photo Themes

| Season | Photo Focus |
|--------|-------------|
| Summer | Stone fruit, berries, salads, outdoor deliveries |
| Autumn | Pumpkins, apples, comfort foods, cozy packaging |
| Winter | Citrus, root vegetables, warming foods, indoor shots |
| Spring | Asparagus, strawberries, fresh greens, bright colors |

## Photo Naming Convention

Use descriptive filenames before upload:
- `rhf-organic-strawberries-mornington-peninsula.jpg`
- `rhf-fresh-produce-delivery-frankston.jpg`
- `rhf-local-dairy-mount-eliza.jpg`

## Customer Photo Strategy

- Monitor tags and mentions
- Ask permission before reposting
- Credit customer in description
- Feature happy delivery moments
- Showcase diverse product usage
- Highlight local community

## Photo Performance Tracking

Monitor weekly:
- Total photo views
- Most viewed photos
- Customer photo submissions
- Photos deleted by Google (flag issues)
- Competitor photo count
- Photo-to-website click rate

## Don't Post

- Low-resolution or blurry images
- Photos with competitor branding visible
- Unflattering or unappealing food shots
- Stock photos (must be authentic)
- Photos with people's faces (without consent)
- Dated or seasonal photos out of season

## Escalation Path

Escalate to Local SEO Team Lead when:
- Photo flagged or removed by Google
- Customer requests photo removal
- Need professional photo shoot
- Seasonal campaign photo needs
- Competitor using our photos

## Tools & Integrations

- Google Business Profile dashboard
- Photo editing tools (Canva, Photoshop)
- Photo storage library (Google Drive/Dropbox)
- Mobile upload app (GBP Manager app)
- Analytics dashboard

## Monthly Review

Audit and optimize:
- Remove photos with <100 views after 90 days
- Refresh seasonal photos
- Check photo-to-profile visit conversion
- Compare to competitor photo counts
- Plan next month's photo themes
