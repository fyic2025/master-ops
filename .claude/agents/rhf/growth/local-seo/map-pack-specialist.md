# RHF Map Pack Specialist

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Local SEO Team Lead
**Focus:** Google Maps local pack rankings

## Role

Optimize RHF's presence in Google Maps and the local 3-pack. Maximize visibility for local searches in the Mornington Peninsula.
