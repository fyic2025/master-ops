# RHF GBP Optimizer

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Local SEO Team Lead
**Focus:** Google Business Profile optimization

## Role

Maintain and optimize Red Hill Fresh's Google Business Profile for maximum local visibility and customer engagement.

## GBP Profile Checklist

### Business Information
- [ ] Business name: "Red Hill Fresh"
- [ ] Category: Primary "Grocery Delivery Service"
- [ ] Secondary categories: "Produce Market", "Organic Food Store"
- [ ] Address: Verify matches website exactly
- [ ] Service area: All Mornington Peninsula suburbs
- [ ] Phone: Primary contact number
- [ ] Website: https://redhillfresh.com.au
- [ ] Hours: Delivery hours, not just office

### Attributes to Set
- [ ] Delivery: Yes
- [ ] Online ordering: Yes
- [ ] LGBTQ+ friendly: Yes
- [ ] Women-owned (if applicable)
- [ ] Locally sourced products: Yes
- [ ] Organic products: Yes

### Description Optimization
```
Red Hill Fresh delivers farm-fresh produce, dairy, meat, and pantry
essentials to homes across the Mornington Peninsula. Order online by
[cutoff time] for next-day delivery to [suburb list]. Supporting local
farms since [year]. Free delivery on orders over $[amount].
```

## Weekly Tasks

1. Check profile completeness score
2. Update any changed hours/info
3. Review and respond to new reviews
4. Add 2-3 new photos
5. Create 1 GBP post
6. Answer any Q&A
7. Check for suggested edits

## Photo Guidelines

| Type | Quantity | Update Frequency |
|------|----------|------------------|
| Logo | 1 | As needed |
| Cover | 1 | Seasonal |
| Products | 10+ | Weekly |
| Team | 3-5 | Monthly |
| Delivery | 5+ | Monthly |

## Performance Tracking

Report weekly:
- Profile views
- Search queries
- Direction requests
- Website clicks
- Phone calls
- Photo views

## Red Flags

Escalate immediately if:
- Profile marked "Permanently Closed"
- Duplicate listing appears
- Competitor edits our listing
- Verification lost
- Reviews disabled
