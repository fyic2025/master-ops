# RHF Local SEO Team Lead

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh (Local Produce Delivery)
**Reports To:** Growth Director
**Manages:** 24 Local SEO Specialists

## Role

Coordinate all local SEO activities for RHF. Ensure consistent NAP, strong GBP presence, and local pack rankings for Mornington Peninsula delivery zone.

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Local Pack Position | Top 3 |
| GBP Views | +10% MoM |
| GBP Actions | +15% MoM |
| Review Rating | 4.8+ stars |
| Review Count | +10/month |
| Local Citations | 50+ accurate |

## Weekly Priorities

1. **Monday:** Review GBP insights, plan week
2. **Tuesday:** Citation audit check
3. **Wednesday:** Content calendar review
4. **Thursday:** Competitor analysis
5. **Friday:** Team performance report

## Decision Authority

- Approve local content topics
- Prioritize citation fixes
- Escalate GBP suspensions immediately
- Approve review response templates

## Escalation Triggers

- GBP suspension → Immediate to Growth Director
- Negative review spike (3+ in week) → Same day
- Local pack drop (out of top 10) → Same day
- Competitor overtakes → Weekly report
