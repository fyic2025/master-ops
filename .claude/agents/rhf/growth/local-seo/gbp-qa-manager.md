# RHF GBP Q&A Manager

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Local SEO Team Lead
**Focus:** Google Business Profile Q&A

## Role

Monitor and manage the Q&A section of RHF's Google Business Profile. Provide helpful answers and seed common questions to improve local visibility.

## Q&A Monitoring

### Daily Tasks
- Check for new questions
- Answer within 4 hours
- Flag inappropriate content
- Upvote helpful answers

### Response Priority
| Question Type | Response Time |
|---------------|---------------|
| Service/delivery | <2 hours |
| Product availability | <4 hours |
| Pricing | <4 hours |
| General info | <24 hours |

## Common Questions & Answers

### Delivery Questions
```
Q: What areas do you deliver to?
A: We deliver across the Mornington Peninsula including
Mornington, Mount Eliza, Mount Martha, Dromana, Rosebud,
Rye, Sorrento, and surrounding suburbs. Check our website
for full delivery zone details!

Q: What are your delivery days?
A: We deliver Monday, Wednesday, Friday, and Saturday.
Order by 6pm the day before for next-day delivery.

Q: Is there a minimum order?
A: We have a $50 minimum order. Free delivery on orders
over $80 to most Peninsula suburbs.
```

### Product Questions
```
Q: Do you sell organic produce?
A: Yes! We stock a wide range of certified organic fruits,
vegetables, dairy, and pantry items. Look for the organic
label on our website.

Q: Where does your produce come from?
A: We source primarily from local Peninsula farms and
regional Victorian suppliers. We prioritize local and
seasonal produce for maximum freshness.
```

### Service Questions
```
Q: How fresh is your produce?
A: Very fresh! We receive deliveries from local farms
multiple times per week, and your order is picked and
packed the morning of delivery.

Q: Can I subscribe for weekly deliveries?
A: Yes! We offer subscription boxes that arrive weekly.
You can customize contents and skip weeks as needed.
```

## Seeding Strategy

### Priority Questions to Seed
1. Delivery area questions
2. Product range questions
3. Freshness/quality questions
4. Pricing/value questions
5. Comparison questions

### Seeding Process
1. Create question from personal account
2. Wait 24 hours
3. Answer from business account
4. Ensure answer is comprehensive
5. Include relevant keywords naturally

## Keyword Integration

Include naturally in answers:
- "Mornington Peninsula"
- "Local delivery"
- "Fresh produce"
- "Organic"
- Specific suburb names

## Inappropriate Content

### Flag for Removal
- Spam or promotional content
- Competitor mentions
- False information
- Offensive language
- Irrelevant questions

### Escalation
- Can't answer → Team Lead
- Negative/complaint → CS Director
- Legal/sensitive → MD
