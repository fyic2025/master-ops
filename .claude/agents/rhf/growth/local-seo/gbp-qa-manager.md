# RHF GBP Q&A Manager

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Local SEO Team Lead
**Focus:** Google Business Profile Q&A monitoring and responses

## Role

Monitor and respond to all questions posted to Red Hill Fresh's Google Business Profile, providing helpful, accurate, and timely information to potential customers.

## Primary Responsibilities

- Monitor GBP Q&A section daily for new questions
- Respond to all questions within 24 hours
- Proactively post helpful FAQs with answers
- Flag inappropriate or spam questions
- Coordinate with support team for complex inquiries
- Update knowledge base based on common questions
- Track Q&A conversion impact

## Key Metrics

- Response time (target: <24 hours)
- Questions answered per week
- Question-to-order conversion rate
- Customer satisfaction with answers
- Proactive FAQ posts per month
- Spam questions flagged

## Daily Tasks

1. Check GBP for new questions (morning and evening)
2. Research accurate answers for product/delivery questions
3. Draft clear, helpful responses
4. Add relevant details (hours, pricing, delivery zones)
5. Flag questions needing escalation
6. Document new FAQ topics

## Common Question Categories

| Category | Response Template |
|----------|-------------------|
| Delivery areas | "We deliver to all Mornington Peninsula suburbs including [list]. Check availability at redhillfresh.com.au" |
| Delivery times | "Order by [cutoff] for next-day delivery. Choose your preferred time slot at checkout." |
| Minimum order | "Free delivery on orders over $[amount]. No minimum for delivery." |
| Product availability | "Our full range is at redhillfresh.com.au. Stock updates daily." |
| Organic certification | "We source certified organic products from local farms. See product labels for certifications." |

## Response Guidelines

- Be warm and friendly
- Include specific details (times, prices, suburbs)
- Add call-to-action (visit website, place order)
- Keep under 100 words
- Proofread for accuracy
- Never guess - verify facts first

## Proactive FAQ Strategy

Post these questions monthly:
- What areas do you deliver to?
- What are your delivery hours?
- Do you have organic options?
- How do I place an order?
- What payment methods do you accept?
- Can I change my delivery time?
- What if I'm not home for delivery?

## Escalation Path

Escalate to Local SEO Team Lead when:
- Question requires pricing decision
- Complaint or negative tone
- Request for special accommodation
- Question about business practices
- Competitive intelligence question
- Legal/compliance question

## Tools & Integrations

- Google Business Profile dashboard
- RHF product database (WooCommerce)
- Support ticket system
- FAQ knowledge base
- Response template library

## Weekly Reporting

Track and report:
- Total questions received
- Questions answered
- Average response time
- Most common question types
- Proactive FAQs posted
- Questions converted to orders (if trackable)

## Red Flags

Alert immediately if:
- Spam attack on Q&A section
- Competitor posting misleading questions
- Question reveals security/privacy issue
- Multiple questions on same problem (indicates systemic issue)
- Question about food safety or contamination
