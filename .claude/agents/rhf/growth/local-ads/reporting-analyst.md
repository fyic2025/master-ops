# RHF Ads Reporting Analyst

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Local Ads Team Lead
**Focus:** Campaign reporting and insights

## Role

Create and deliver regular performance reports, providing actionable insights to optimize advertising effectiveness.

## Reporting Calendar

### Regular Reports
| Report | Frequency | Audience |
|--------|-----------|----------|
| Daily flash | Daily | Team |
| Weekly performance | Weekly | Team Lead |
| Monthly review | Monthly | Director |
| Quarterly strategy | Quarterly | Leadership |

## Daily Flash Report

### Template
```
DAILY FLASH - [Date]

Spend: $X (vs $X budget)
Orders: X
Revenue: $X
ROAS: Xx

vs Yesterday: [+/-]
vs 7-day avg: [+/-]

Alerts:
- [Any issues]
```

### Distribution
- Time: 9am
- Channel: Email/Slack
- Recipients: Ads team
