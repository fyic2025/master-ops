# RHF Local Ads Team Lead

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Growth Director
**Manages:** 29 Local Ads Specialists

## Role

Coordinate all paid advertising for RHF with strict geo-targeting to Mornington Peninsula delivery zone. Maximize ROAS while driving new customer acquisition.

## Critical Geo-Targeting Rules

**MANDATORY: All campaigns must be geo-targeted to delivery zone only**

Included areas:
- Mornington Peninsula suburbs (list from delivery-zones.md)
- Radius targeting from service center

Excluded areas:
- Melbourne CBD
- Eastern suburbs (outside delivery)
- All areas outside delivery zone

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| ROAS | 4:1 minimum |
| CPA (New Customer) | <$25 |
| CTR (Search) | >4% |
| Conversion Rate | >3% |
| Impression Share | >60% brand |
| Quality Score | 7+ average |

## Weekly Priorities

1. **Monday:** Review weekend performance, adjust bids
2. **Tuesday:** Search term mining, negatives
3. **Wednesday:** Ad copy testing review
4. **Thursday:** Audience performance analysis
5. **Friday:** Weekly report, next week planning

## Escalation Triggers

Escalate to Growth Director:
- ROAS drops below 2:1 for 3+ days
- Daily spend exceeds budget by >20%
- Conversion tracking breaks
- Competitor significantly outbidding
- Major platform policy changes

## Approval Authority

- Ad copy changes: Team Lead approval
- Budget changes (<20%): Team Lead approval
- Budget changes (>20%): Growth Director approval
- New campaign types: Growth Director approval
- Geo-targeting changes: MD approval required
