## Allocation Principles

### Budget by Funnel Stage
| Stage | Purpose | % Budget |
|-------|---------|----------|
| Awareness | New audience reach | 10% |
| Consideration | Generic searches | 40% |
| Intent | Product/brand searches | 35% |
| Retention | Remarketing | 15% |

### Performance-Based Allocation
```
Increase budget when:
- ROAS > target by 20%+
- CPA below target
- Impression share limited by budget
- Conversion volume growing

Decrease budget when:
- ROAS below target
- CPA above target
- Quality issues
- Declining conversion rates
```

## Pacing Management

### Daily Pacing Check
```
Expected spend (day X of month):
= Monthly budget × (Day / Days in month)

Variance threshold: ±10%

If over-pacing: Review high-spend campaigns
If under-pacing: Check for issues, increase bids
```

## Reallocation Triggers

### Move Budget TO Campaign When:
| Condition | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| Limited by budget + ROAS >500% | Increase 20% |
| Limited by budget + CPA <target | Increase 15% |
| Strong conversion growth | Increase 10% |

### Move Budget FROM Campaign When:
| Condition | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| ROAS <200% for 7 days | Decrease 20% |
| CPA >2x target | Decrease 25% |
| Declining conversions | Decrease 10% |

## Budget Change Process

### Small Changes (<15%)
1. Identify need
2. Make adjustment
3. Document in log
4. Monitor impact

### Large Changes (>15%)
1. Identify need
2. Get Team Lead approval
3. Make adjustment
4. Document reasoning
5. Monitor closely for 7 days

## Escalation

Alert Team Lead if:
- Overspend >20% of daily budget
- Major campaign depleting budget early
- Unable to spend budget (quality issues)
- Need >25% budget change
