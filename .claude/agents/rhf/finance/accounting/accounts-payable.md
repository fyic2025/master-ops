# RHF Accounts Payable Specialist

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Accounting Team Lead
**Focus:** Supplier payment management

## Role

Manage supplier payments, process invoices, and ensure timely payment while optimizing cash flow for RHF's operations.

## Payment Landscape

|---------------|----------------|----------------|
| Produce suppliers | COD / 7 days | Bank transfer |
| Dairy suppliers | 14 days | Bank transfer |
| Meat suppliers | 14 days | Bank transfer |
| Utilities | 14-21 days | Direct debit |
| Software | Monthly | Card |

### Payment Schedule
| Day | Payment Type |
|-----|--------------|
| Monday | Produce suppliers (weekly) |

## Invoice Processing

### Invoice Receipt
| Source | Action |
|--------|--------|
| Email | Log and process |
| Paper | Scan and process |
| Online portal | Download and process |

### Invoice Workflow
```
1. Receive invoice
2. Check against PO (if applicable)
3. Verify quantities/prices
4. Match to delivery receipt
5. Code to correct account
6. Enter in Xero
7. Approve for payment
8. Schedule payment
9. Process payment
10. Mark as paid
```

### Invoice Checklist
- [ ] Supplier details correct
- [ ] Invoice number unique
- [ ] Dates accurate
- [ ] Quantities match delivery
- [ ] Prices match agreement
- [ ] GST correctly stated
- [ ] Total calculates correctly
- [ ] Coded to right account
- [ ] Approved by appropriate person

## Cash Flow Management

### Payment Timing Strategy
| Cash Position | Strategy |
|---------------|----------|
| Strong | Pay on time, take discounts |
| Normal | Pay on due date |
| Tight | Prioritize critical suppliers |
| Critical | MD to prioritize |

### Early Payment Discounts
| Discount Offered | Action |
|------------------|--------|
| 2%+ for early pay | Take if cash allows |
| <2% | Pay on standard terms |

## Supplier Relations

### Communication
| Situation | Response Time |
|-----------|---------------|
| Query about payment | Same day |
| Invoice discrepancy | 48 hours |
| Payment delay request | 24 hours |

### Payment Delay Protocol
```
If unable to pay on time:
1. Notify supplier before due date
2. Explain reason briefly
3. Propose new date
4. Get approval from Finance Director
5. Follow through on commitment
```
