## Commentary Guidelines

### Effective Commentary
```
Include:
- What happened (facts)
- Why it happened (analysis)
- What it means (impact)
- What we'll do (action)

Avoid:
- Just restating numbers
- Missing key drivers
- Vague explanations
- No action items
```

## Quality Checks

### Report Checklist
```
Before finalizing:
□ Numbers tie to source
□ Formulas correct
□ Comparatives accurate
□ Commentary complete
□ Formatting consistent
□ Proofread
□ Approved by Lead
```
