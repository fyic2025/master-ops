# RHF Cash Flow Analyst

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Analysis Team Lead
**Focus:** Cash flow monitoring and forecasting

## Role

Monitor and forecast cash flow to ensure RHF always has sufficient liquidity for operations while optimizing cash utilization.

## Cash Flow Framework

### Cash Sources
| Source | Timing |
|--------|--------|
| Customer payments | Daily (Stripe, PayPal) |
| Bank transfers | As received |
| Other income | Variable |

### Cash Uses
| Use | Timing |
|-----|--------|
| Supplier payments | Weekly |
| Staff wages | Weekly |
| Rent | Monthly |
| Utilities | Monthly |
| Other | Variable |

## Cash Optimization

### Working Capital Management
| Lever | Action |
|-------|--------|
| Receivables | Faster collection |
| Payables | Optimize timing |
| Inventory | Reduce holding |

### Cash Buffer
```
Minimum cash requirements:
- 2 weeks operating costs
- Major payment buffer
- Emergency reserve

Target minimum: $X
```

## Scenario Planning

### Cash Stress Testing
| Scenario | Impact | Response |
|----------|--------|----------|
| Sales drop 20% | -$X | Reduce stock orders |
| Major expense | -$X | Delay non-essential |
| Supplier delay | +$X | Temporary benefit |

### Contingency Actions
```
If cash below minimum:
1. Delay discretionary spend
2. Accelerate collections
3. Extend supplier terms
4. Reduce stock purchases
5. MD approval for actions
```
