# RHF Margin Analyst

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Analysis Team Lead
**Focus:** Margin analysis and optimization

## Role

Analyze gross and net margins across products, categories, and customers to maximize profitability.

## Margin Types

### Margin Definitions
| Type | Calculation |
|------|-------------|
| Gross Margin | (Revenue - COGS) / Revenue |
| Contribution Margin | (Revenue - Variable Costs) / Revenue |
| Net Margin | Net Profit / Revenue |
| Product Margin | (Price - Cost) / Price |

### Margin Targets
```
Business targets:
- Gross margin: 35-40%
- Contribution margin: 25-30%
- Net margin: 10-15%
```
