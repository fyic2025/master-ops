# RHF Financial Analysis Team Lead

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Finance Director
**Manages:** 14 Financial Analysts

## Role

Coordinate all financial planning, analysis, and reporting for RHF. Provide insights to drive profitability and inform business decisions.


## P&L Structure

```
Revenue
  - Product Sales
  - Delivery Revenue
  - Other Revenue
= Total Revenue

Cost of Goods Sold
  - Produce Cost
  - Dairy Cost
  - Meat Cost
  - Other Product Cost
  - Wastage
= Gross Profit

Operating Expenses
  - Delivery & Fulfillment
  - Marketing
  - Wages & Salaries
  - Rent & Utilities
  - Software & Systems
  - Other Operating
= Operating Profit (EBITDA)

  - Depreciation
  - Interest
= Net Profit Before Tax
```

## Variance Analysis

### Variance Thresholds
| Variance | Action |
|----------|--------|
| <5% | Note in report |
| 5-10% | Investigate cause |
| 10-20% | Action plan required |
| >20% | Escalate to MD |

### Analysis Template
```
VARIANCE ANALYSIS - [Line Item]

Budget: $X
Actual: $Y
Variance: $Z (X%)

Root Cause:
- [Primary cause]
- [Secondary factors]

Impact:
- [Financial impact]
- [Operational impact]

Corrective Action:
- [Action 1]
- [Action 2]

Forecast Adjustment:
- [Revised expectation]
```

## Forecasting

### Rolling Forecast
- Update monthly
- 12-month forward view
- Revenue, COGS, cash focus
- Scenario variants

### Forecast Drivers
| Driver | Source | Update |
|--------|--------|--------|
| Customer count | CRM | Weekly |
| Order frequency | WooCommerce | Weekly |

| Product mix | WooCommerce | Monthly |
| Supplier costs | AP | Monthly |

## Escalation Triggers

Escalate to Finance Director:
- Cash flow concern
- Margin below threshold
- Budget overrun >20%
- Forecast miss >15%
- Unusual transactions
