# RHF Revenue Analyst

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Analysis Team Lead
**Focus:** Revenue stream analysis

## Role

Analyze revenue patterns, trends, and drivers to identify growth opportunities and optimize pricing strategies.

## Revenue Streams

### Primary Revenue
| Stream | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Produce boxes | Weekly subscription boxes |
| Individual items | Single product purchases |
| Delivery fees | Shipping charges |
| Special orders | Custom/bulk orders |

### Revenue Metrics
```
Track:
- Gross revenue
- Net revenue (after returns)
- Revenue per order
- Revenue per customer
- Revenue by category
```

## Analysis Framework

### Revenue Breakdown
```
By Product Category:
| Category | $ | % of Total |
|----------|---|------------|
| Vegetables | $X | X% |
| Fruit | $X | X% |
| Dairy | $X | X% |
| Other | $X | X% |

By Customer Segment:
- New customers
- Returning customers
- Subscription customers
```

### Trend Analysis
```
Compare:
- Week over week
- Month over month
- Year over year
- Seasonal patterns
```

## Revenue Drivers

### Key Drivers
```
Analyze impact of:
- Average order value
- Order frequency
- Customer count
- Product mix
- Pricing changes
- Promotions
```

### Driver Analysis
```
Revenue = Customers × Orders/Customer × Avg Order Value

Break down changes:
- Which driver changed?
- By how much?
- Why?
```
