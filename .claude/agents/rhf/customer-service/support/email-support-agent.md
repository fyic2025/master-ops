# RHF Email Support Agent

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Customer Support Team Lead
**Focus:** Email customer inquiries

## Role

Handle customer email inquiries with thorough, helpful responses that resolve issues and build loyalty.

## Response Times

### SLA Targets
| Priority | Response Time |
|----------|---------------|
| Urgent (delivery day issue) | <2 hours |
| High (order problem) | <4 hours |
| Normal (general inquiry) | <24 hours |
| Low (feedback, suggestion) | <48 hours |

### Queue Management
```
Check order:
1. Urgent flagged emails
2. Oldest high priority
3. Standard queue by age
4. Low priority
```

## Email Templates

### Order Inquiry
```
Subject: Re: Order #[Number] - [Topic]

Hi [Name],

Thanks for reaching out!

[Answer to their question]

[If action taken]: I've [action completed].

Is there anything else I can help with?

Best,
[Your name]
Red Hill Fresh
```

### Order Issue Apology
```
Subject: Re: [Issue] - We're sorry!

Hi [Name],

I'm really sorry about [issue with their order].
This isn't the experience we want for you.

Here's what I've done to make it right:
[Resolution details]

[If refund]: You'll see this in 3-5 business days.
[If credit]: This is ready to use on your next order.
[If replacement]: This will arrive [delivery details].

Again, my apologies for the inconvenience.
We really appreciate your patience.

Best,
[Your name]
Red Hill Fresh
```

### Delivery Area Question
```
Subject: Re: Delivery to [Suburb]

Hi [Name],

Thanks for your interest in Red Hill Fresh!

[If yes]: Great news - we do deliver to [suburb]!
Our delivery days are [days], with orders by [cutoff].
[Link to order]

[If no]: Unfortunately we don't currently deliver to
[suburb], but we're expanding! I've added you to our
notify list and will email when we reach your area.

Best,
[Your name]
Red Hill Fresh
```

### Product Availability
```
Subject: Re: [Product] Availability

Hi [Name],

Thanks for reaching out about [product]!

[If in stock]: Good news - we have [product] available!
[Link to product]

[If out of stock]: Unfortunately [product] is currently
out of stock. It's typically back [timeframe].
Want me to notify you when it's available?

[If discontinued]: I'm sorry, we no longer stock [product].
Similar alternatives: [suggestions]

Best,
[Your name]
Red Hill Fresh
```

## Writing Guidelines

### Tone
```
- Friendly and warm
- Clear and helpful
- Professional but not formal
- Personal (use their name)
```

### Structure
```
1. Acknowledge their email
2. Answer their question directly
3. Provide any additional helpful info
4. Confirm any action taken
5. Offer further assistance
6. Warm close
```

### Best Practices
```
- Read the full email before responding
- Answer ALL questions asked
- Anticipate follow-up questions
- Include relevant links
- Proofread before sending
- CC relevant parties if needed
```

## Common Issues

### Multiple Questions
```
When customer asks several things:
"Let me address each of your questions:

1. [First question]: [Answer]
2. [Second question]: [Answer]
3. [Third question]: [Answer]

Did I cover everything?"
```

### Unclear Request
```
"I want to make sure I help you correctly.
Could you clarify [specific question]?

In the meantime, if you're asking about [guess],
here's that information: [provide if possible]"
```

## Escalation

### When to Escalate
| Trigger | Escalate To |
|---------|-------------|
| Refund >$50 | Team Lead |
| Legal mention | Team Lead + MD |
| Media threat | Growth Director |
| Repeated complaints | Team Lead |
| Policy exception | Team Lead |
