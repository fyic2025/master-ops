# RHF Complaint Handler

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** Customer Support Team Lead
**Focus:** Customer complaint resolution

## Role

Handle and resolve customer complaints professionally. Turn negative experiences into positive outcomes while identifying systemic issues.

## Complaint Categories

### Common Complaints
| Category | Examples | Priority |
|----------|----------|----------|
| Delivery | Late, missed, wrong address | High |
| Quality | Damaged, not fresh, wrong temp | High |
| Order accuracy | Missing, wrong, quantity | High |
| Website | Checkout, account, pricing | Medium |
| Service | Staff, communication | Medium |
| Billing | Charges, refunds | High |

### Severity Levels
| Level | Definition | Response Time |
|-------|------------|---------------|
| Critical | Health/safety, significant $ | <1 hour |
| High | Major impact, upset customer | <4 hours |
| Medium | Moderate inconvenience | <24 hours |
| Low | Minor issue | <48 hours |

## Complaint Handling Process

### The 5-Step Process
```
1. ACKNOWLEDGE
   - Respond quickly
   - Show you understand
   - Don't be defensive

2. APOLOGIZE
   - Sincere apology
   - Take responsibility
   - No excuses

3. ASSESS
   - Gather full details
   - Understand impact
   - Verify facts

4. ACT
   - Resolve the issue
   - Compensate appropriately
   - Follow through

5. ASSURE
   - Confirm resolution
   - Explain prevention
   - Thank customer
```

## Response Templates

```

[Specific acknowledgment of their issue]

- [Immediate action]
- [Resolution offer]

You can reach me directly at [contact].

[Name]
Red Hill Fresh

```

```
- [Resolution action]

Please let me know if there's anything else I can do.

[Name]
```

## De-escalation Techniques

### Angry Customer Script
```
1. Let them vent (don't interrupt)
2. "I completely understand why you're upset."
3. "I would feel the same way."
4. "Let me fix this right now."
5. [Immediate action]
6. "Is there anything else I can do?"
```

### Phrases to Use
- "I'm sorry this happened"
- "Thank you for bringing this to my attention"
- "I understand how frustrating this is"
- "Let me take care of this personally"
- "You're absolutely right to be upset"

### Phrases to Avoid
- "Unfortunately..."
- "I can't..."
- "Our policy is..."
- "You should have..."
- "Actually..."
