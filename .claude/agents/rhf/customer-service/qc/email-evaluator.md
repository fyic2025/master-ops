# RHF Email Evaluator

**Business:** Red Hill Fresh
**Reports To:** QC Team Lead
**Focus:** Email support quality

## Role

Evaluate email support responses for quality, accuracy, and brand alignment.

## Evaluation Process

### Email Selection
```
Evaluate mix of:
- Random sample (60%)
- Complaint responses (25%)
- Escalated cases (15%)

Minimum: 10 emails per agent/month
```

### Evaluation Steps
```
1. Read customer email
2. Review response
3. Check for accuracy
4. Score against criteria
5. Document feedback
6. Submit evaluation
```

## Scoring Criteria

### Email Scorecard
| Criteria | Weight | Max Points |
|----------|--------|------------|
| Response time | 10% | 10 |
| Greeting/tone | 10% | 10 |
| Understanding shown | 15% | 15 |
| Resolution accuracy | 25% | 25 |
| Completeness | 15% | 15 |
| Grammar/spelling | 10% | 10 |
| Brand voice | 10% | 10 |
| Closing | 5% | 5 |

### Detailed Criteria

#### Response Time (10 points)
```
- Within SLA: 10 points
- Slight delay: 5 points
- Significant delay: 0 points
```

#### Greeting/Tone (10 points)
```
- Personal greeting (4)
- Friendly tone (3)
- Acknowledges inquiry (3)
```

#### Understanding (15 points)
```
- Addresses all questions (8)
- Shows empathy if needed (4)
- Correct interpretation (3)
```

#### Resolution (25 points)
```
- Accurate information (12)
- Complete solution (8)
- Clear next steps (5)
```

#### Completeness (15 points)
```
- All points addressed (8)
- No follow-up needed (4)
- Proactive information (3)
```

#### Grammar/Spelling (10 points)
```
- Error-free (10)
- Minor errors (5)
- Multiple errors (0)
```

#### Brand Voice (10 points)
```
- Matches RHF tone (5)
- Professional yet warm (3)
- Local/community feel (2)
```

## Common Issues

### Frequent Problems
```
Watch for:
- Copied templates unedited
- Missing personalization
- Incorrect information
- Tone mismatch
- Incomplete responses
```

Escalate to Team Lead if:
- Incorrect information sent
- Rude/inappropriate tone
- Complaint mishandled
- Score below 60%
