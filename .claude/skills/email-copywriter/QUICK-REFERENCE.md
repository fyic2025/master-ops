# Email Copywriter - Quick Reference

## Subject Line Limits

| Client | Desktop | Mobile | Best Practice |
|--------|---------|--------|---------------|
| Gmail | 70 chars | 40 chars | <50 chars |
| Outlook | 60 chars | 35 chars | <50 chars |
| Apple Mail | 70 chars | 35 chars | <50 chars |

---

## Preview Text

- Length: 40-90 characters
- Don't repeat subject
- Add value/curiosity
- Complete the thought

---

## Email Type Timing

| Flow | Email 1 | Email 2 | Email 3 |
|------|---------|---------|---------|
| Welcome | Immediate | Day 2 | Day 4-7 |
| Abandoned Cart | 1 hour | 24 hours | 48-72 hours |
| Post-Purchase | Immediate | Shipping | Day 7 |
| Winback | 30-60 days | +3 days | +7 days |

## Business Voice Quick Guide

**BOO:** Warm, educational, wellness-focused
**Teelixir:** Inspiring, transformative, premium
**Elevate:** Professional, B2B, value-focused
**RHF:** Friendly, local, community

---

## Subject Line Formulas

```
Benefit + Curiosity:
"The secret to [benefit]"

Question:
"Ready to [achieve goal]?"

How-To:
"How to [benefit] with [product]"

List:
"5 ways to [benefit]"

Urgency:
"Last chance: [offer] ends tonight"

Personalized:
"[Name], your [product] is waiting"
```

---

## Strong CTAs

- Shop Now
- Get Started
- Claim Offer
- Order Today
- View Collection

**Avoid:** Click Here, Submit, Learn More

---

## Email Structure

```
1. Subject (hook)
2. Preview (expand)
3. Header (brand)
4. Opening (hook)
5. Body (value)
6. CTA (action)
7. Footer (legal)
```

---

## Copy Guidelines

- Paragraphs: 2-3 sentences max
- Bullets for features
- Bold key info
- Benefits over features
- Australian English
- No ALL CAPS

---

## Compliance Checklist

```
[ ] Sender name visible
[ ] Physical address included
[ ] Unsubscribe link clear
[ ] Subject not misleading
[ ] Consent obtained
```
