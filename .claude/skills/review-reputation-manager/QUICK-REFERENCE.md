## Google Business Profile

### Quick Actions
- Respond to reviews
- Update photos
- Answer Q&A
- Post updates
- Monitor insights

### Don't Do
- Incentivize reviews
- Gate reviews (ask happy first)
- Argue with reviewers
- Ignore negative reviews
- Fake reviews

## Product Review Platforms

### BigCommerce (BOO)
```
Endpoint: /catalog/products/{id}/reviews
Status: pending | approved | disapproved
```

### Judge.me (Teelixir)
```
Endpoint: https://judge.me/api/v1/reviews
Features: Photo reviews, video, Q&A
```

### WooCommerce (RHF)
```
Native WordPress comments
Plugin: WooCommerce Product Reviews
```
