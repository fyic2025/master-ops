# Review Response Playbook

## Response Framework

### The HEART Method

```
H - Hear the customer (acknowledge their feedback)
E - Empathize with their experience
A - Apologize if warranted
R - Resolve or offer to resolve
T - Thank them for the feedback
```

## 3-Star Review Responses

### Goals
- Thank for honest feedback
- Express desire to improve
- Move conversation offline
- Show you take feedback seriously

### Templates

Sincerely,
[Business Name]

**Template 2 - With Specific Issue:**
```

Would you mind emailing us at [email]? We'd appreciate the chance to turn this around.

Best,
The [Business] Team
```

## Negative Review Responses (1-2 Stars)

### Goals
- Respond quickly (within 4 hours if possible)
- Show empathy and accountability
- Take conversation offline
- Demonstrate commitment to resolution
- Never argue or be defensive

### Step-by-Step Process

```
1. Take a breath - don't respond emotionally
2. Investigate the issue internally first
3. Draft response following templates
4. Have second person review if possible
5. Post response
6. Follow up privately with solution
7. Document for process improvement
```

### Templates

**Template 1 - Service Issue:**
```
[Name], we're truly sorry to hear about your experience.

This is not the level of service we strive for, and we understand your frustration. We take this feedback very seriously.

We would like to make this right. Please contact us directly at [email] or [phone] so we can resolve this for you personally.

Thank you for bringing this to our attention.

Sincerely,
[Name/Title]
[Business Name]
```

**Template 2 - Product Issue:**
```
We're sorry to hear [product] didn't meet your expectations, [Name].

We stand behind our products and want to make this right. Your satisfaction is genuinely important to us.

Please reach out to our team at [email] with your order details, and we'll do everything we can to resolve this situation.

With apologies,
The [Business] Team
```

**Template 3 - Shipping/Delivery Issue:**
```
[Name], we sincerely apologize for the shipping issues you experienced.

We understand how frustrating this must be, and this isn't the experience we want for our customers.

We're looking into what happened with your order. Please contact us at [email] with your order number so we can address this directly and ensure it doesn't happen again.

Thank you for your patience.

[Business Name]
```

**Template 4 - Customer Error (Handle Carefully):**
```
Thank you for your feedback, [Name].

We're sorry to hear things didn't work out as expected. We'd love to help clarify [the issue] and find a solution that works for you.

Please reach out to us at [email] - we're here to help!

Best regards,
[Business Name]
```

### What NOT to Do

```
‚ùå Argue or be defensive
‚ùå Blame the customer
‚ùå Make excuses
‚ùå Copy-paste without personalization
‚ùå Offer incentives publicly
‚ùå Ignore the review
‚ùå Respond emotionally
‚ùå Share private information
‚ùå Ask them to change/remove review
```

## Special Situations

### Suspected Fake Review

```
Response:
"We take all feedback seriously and have searched our records but cannot find an order or interaction matching this description. If you could contact us at [email] with your order details, we'd like to investigate further and address any concerns.

Thank you,
[Business Name]"

Action:
- Document evidence
- Report to platform if clear violation
- Don't accuse publicly
```

### Competitor Attack

```
Response:
(Same as fake review response)

Action:
- Screenshot evidence
- Report to platform
- Consider legal advice if pattern continues
- Document everything
```

### Review Mentions Employee by Name

**Positive:**
```
"Thank you for the wonderful review, [Name]! We'll make sure to pass along your kind words to [Employee] - they'll be thrilled! Our team takes pride in providing great service, and your feedback means a lot. Thank you for choosing us!"
```

**Negative:**
```
"Thank you for your feedback, [Name]. We're sorry your experience didn't meet expectations. We take all feedback seriously and will address this internally. Please reach out to us at [email] so we can make things right. We appreciate you bringing this to our attention."

(Handle employee situation privately, don't discuss publicly)
```

### Review is Unclear/Vague

```
"Thank you for taking the time to leave feedback, [Name]. We'd love to understand more about your experience so we can ensure we're always improving. If you wouldn't mind sharing a few more details at [email], we'd really appreciate it. Thanks for choosing [Business]!"
```

## Response Timing Guidelines

| Review Type | Target Response Time |
|-------------|---------------------|
| 1-2 Stars | Within 4 hours |
| 3 Stars | Within 24 hours |
| 4 Stars | Within 48 hours |
| 5 Stars | Within 48 hours |

### Priority Queue

```

1. Any new 1-2 star reviews ‚Üí Respond immediately

```

```

If there's anything else we can do, please don't hesitate to reach out directly.

Thank you for giving us the opportunity to make things right.

Warm regards,
[Your Name]
[Title]
[Business Name]
```

### After Successful Resolution

```
(Only if customer seems open to it)

"We're so glad we could resolve this for you, [Name]. If you ever feel inclined to update your review based on your experience, we'd be grateful - but no pressure at all! Thank you again for your patience and for being a valued customer."
```
