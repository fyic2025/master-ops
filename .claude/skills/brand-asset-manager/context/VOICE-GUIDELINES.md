# Brand Voice Guidelines

Comprehensive voice and tone guidelines for all 4 businesses. Use these when writing copy, emails, product descriptions, or any customer-facing content.

## Teelixir Voice

### Personality Traits
1. **Expert** - Deep knowledge of tonic herbs and adaptogens
2. **Passionate** - Genuine enthusiasm for wellness
3. **Premium** - Quality-focused, not mass-market
4. **Mystical** - Honoring ancient traditions

### Tone Characteristics
| Attribute | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Tone | Inspiring, knowledgeable, passionate |
| Language | Elevated but accessible, wellness-focused |
| Perspective | Educational expert sharing wisdom |
| Expertise | Deep authority on functional mushrooms/adaptogens |

### Writing Guidelines

**DO:**
- Reference traditional use and history
- Explain dual-extraction and quality processes
- Use proper terminology (adaptogens, tonic herbs)
- Connect to modern wellness goals
- Highlight potency and bioavailability
- Create desire for transformation

**DON'T:**
- Make therapeutic claims
- Oversimplify (audience is knowledgeable)
- Use generic health food language
- Ignore sourcing/quality story
- Be boring or clinical

### Key Terminology
- **Adaptogens** - Help body adapt to stress
- **Tonic herbs** - Traditional herbs for vitality
- **Dual-extracted** - Water and alcohol extraction
- **Bioavailable** - Body can absorb and use
- **Qi** - Vital energy (TCM)
- **Jing** - Essence/life force
- **Shen** - Spirit/consciousness

### Example Phrases
- "Harness the power of..."
- "Used for centuries in traditional medicine..."
- "Support your body's natural ability to..."
- "Experience the transformative benefits..."
- "Potent, pure, and powerful..."

## Buy Organics Online Voice

### Personality Traits
1. **Trustworthy** - We're experts you can rely on
2. **Supportive** - We help you on your wellness journey
3. **Knowledgeable** - We understand organic and health products
4. **Accessible** - Premium doesn't mean pretentious

### Tone Characteristics
| Attribute | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Tone | Warm, informative, encouraging |
| Language | Clear, educational, jargon-free |
| Perspective | "We" and "you" - conversational |
| Expertise | Confident but not condescending |

### Writing Guidelines

**DO:**
- Lead with benefits to the customer
- Explain what makes products special
- Use Australian spelling and terms
- Include serving/usage suggestions
- Mention certifications prominently
- Connect products to lifestyle goals

**DON'T:**
- Use medical claims
- Be overly salesy or pushy
- Assume prior knowledge
- Use complex scientific terms without explanation
- Copy supplier descriptions verbatim

### Example Phrases
- "Nourish your body with..."
- "A natural choice for..."
- "Sourced from trusted suppliers..."
- "Supporting your wellness journey..."
- "Quality you can trust..."

## Elevate Wholesale Voice

### Personality Traits
1. **Professional** - Business-to-business expertise
2. **Supportive** - Partner in your success
3. **Reliable** - Consistent quality and supply
4. **Value-focused** - Help you maximize margins

### Tone Characteristics
| Attribute | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Tone | Professional, helpful, business-like |
| Language | Clear, efficient, benefit-focused |
| Perspective | Business partner perspective |
| Expertise | Retail and wholesale expertise |

### Writing Guidelines

**DO:**
- Focus on retail benefits (margins, turnover)
- Mention customer demand/popularity
- Include practical details (shelf life, MOQ)
- Highlight marketing support available
- Use business metrics where relevant
- Position as partnership opportunity

**DON'T:**
- Use consumer marketing language
- Ignore commercial realities
- Be overly casual
- Forget decision-makers are businesses
- Skip practical ordering information

### Key Information to Include
- Wholesale price / RRP
- Margin potential
- Minimum order quantity
- Shelf life
- Best-selling status
- Marketing materials available
- Reorder frequency

### Example Phrases
- "A proven seller that delivers..."
- "Stock your shelves with customer favourites..."
- "Competitive wholesale pricing..."
- "Marketing support included..."
- "Partner with us for..."

## Red Hill Fresh Voice

### Personality Traits
1. **Local** - Proudly Mornington Peninsula
2. **Fresh** - Quality you can taste
3. **Community** - Supporting local farmers
4. **Family** - Wholesome, accessible

### Tone Characteristics
| Attribute | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Tone | Friendly, warm, authentic |
| Language | Simple, honest, down-to-earth |
| Perspective | Neighbor sharing good finds |
| Expertise | Local knowledge, farm connections |

### Writing Guidelines

**DO:**
- Mention local sourcing when applicable
- Include farm/producer names when known
- Emphasize freshness and seasonality
- Connect to local community
- Use friendly, conversational tone
- Suggest family-friendly uses

**DON'T:**
- Use corporate language
- Be impersonal
- Ignore the local story
- Use generic supermarket descriptions
- Forget seasonal availability notes

### Example Phrases
- "Straight from local farms..."
- "Fresh from the Mornington Peninsula..."
- "Supporting local growers..."
- "A family favourite..."
- "Picked fresh and delivered to you..."

## Universal Guidelines

### Australian English
- Use Australian spelling (colour, favour, organise)
- Use metric measurements
- Reference Australian seasons correctly
- Use local terminology (capsicums not peppers)

### General Writing Rules
- Write for humans first, SEO second
- Use active voice
- Break up long text with bullets/headers
- Include clear calls to action
- Proofread everything

### What to Avoid (All Brands)
- Unsubstantiated claims
- ALL CAPS for emphasis
- Excessive exclamation marks!!!
- Plagiarizing competitor descriptions
- Overly long paragraphs (mobile readers)
