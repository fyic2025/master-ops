## Carousel Structure

### Product Carousel (5-7 slides)
1. Hero image with product
2. Key benefit #1
3. Key benefit #2
4. How to use
5. Social proof
6. Ingredients
7. CTA + price

### Educational (5-10 slides)
1. Hook question
2. Introduction
3-7. Main points
8. Summary
9. CTA
10. Save/share prompt
