# Keyword Strategy Guide

## Keyword Research Process

### Step 1: Seed Keywords

Start with core business terms:

**BOO:**
- organic food, supplements, superfoods
- health food online, natural products
- organic store australia

**Teelixir:**
- mushroom extract, adaptogens, tonic herbs
- reishi, lion's mane, chaga, cordyceps
- functional mushrooms australia

**Elevate:**
- wholesale health food, organic wholesale
- health food distributor, retail supplier
- wholesale supplements australia

**RHF:**
- fresh produce delivery, local produce
- mornington peninsula groceries
- farm fresh delivery melbourne

### Step 2: Keyword Expansion

For each seed keyword, find:
- Long-tail variations
- Question keywords (how, what, why, where)
- Comparison keywords (vs, alternative, best)
- Commercial keywords (buy, shop, order)

### Step 3: Search Intent Classification

| Intent | Keywords Include | Content Type |
|--------|-----------------|--------------|
| Informational | how, what, why, guide | Blog, guides |
| Commercial | best, top, review | Comparisons, reviews |
| Transactional | buy, order, shop, price | Product pages |
| Navigational | brand, near me, login | Landing pages |

## Keyword Prioritization Matrix

### Priority Score Calculation

```
Score = (Volume × 0.3) + (Relevance × 0.4) + (Competition × 0.3)

Volume Score (1-5):
- <100: 1
- 100-500: 2
- 500-1000: 3
- 1000-5000: 4
- 5000+: 5

Relevance Score (1-5):
- Not relevant: 1
- Somewhat relevant: 2
- Relevant: 3
- Very relevant: 4
- Perfect match: 5

Competition Score (1-5):
- Very high: 1
- High: 2
- Medium: 3
- Low: 4
- Very low: 5
```

## Content-Keyword Mapping

### Blog Content Keywords

**Quick Wins (Low competition, high relevance):**
- Long-tail question keywords
- Specific product comparisons
- Local + product combinations
- "How to use [product]" variations

**Authority Building (Higher competition):**
- Broad category terms
- Pillar content keywords
- Industry terminology
- Brand + category keywords

### Product Page Keywords

**Primary Keywords:**
- Product name + type
- Brand + product
- Category + specific product

**Secondary Keywords:**
- Benefits keywords
- Use case keywords
- Comparison keywords
- Alternative product keywords

## Competitor Keyword Gaps

### Analysis Process

1. Identify top 5 competitors per business
2. Export their ranking keywords
3. Filter for relevant terms we don't rank for
4. Prioritize by volume and difficulty
5. Create content plan to capture gaps

### Common Gap Opportunities

| Gap Type | Example | Action |
|----------|---------|--------|
| Missing content | Topic we haven't covered | Create new content |
| Under-optimized | We rank page 2-3 | Optimize existing |
| Better content | Competitor ranks higher | Improve our content |
| New trends | Emerging keywords | Early mover content |
