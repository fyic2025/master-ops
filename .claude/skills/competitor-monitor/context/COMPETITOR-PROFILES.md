# Competitor Profiles

## BOO Competitors

### iHerb

**Overview:**
- Global health products marketplace
- US-based, ships internationally
- Massive product range (30,000+ products)

**Positioning:** Low price, wide selection, international brands

**Strengths:**
- Extremely competitive pricing (often 30-50% lower)
- Huge product range
- Fast international shipping
- Well-established trust

**Weaknesses:**
- Not Australian owned
- International shipping times
- Customer service challenges
- Import restrictions on some products

**Key Metrics:**
- Traffic: Very high
- Price position: Budget leader
- Delivery: 1-2 weeks typically

**Threat Level:** HIGH (price competition)

---
