## Monitoring Framework

### Price Monitoring

**Weekly Price Checks:**
```
Priority Products:
- Top 50 SKUs by revenue
- Top 20 traffic drivers
- Price-sensitive categories
- New product launches

Data to Capture:
- Regular price
- Sale price (if any)
- Shipping cost/threshold
- Bundle pricing
- Promotion details
```

**Monthly Price Analysis:**
```
Report Components:
- Price position by category
- Price gap analysis
- Trend over time
- Competitive index score
```

### Product Range Monitoring

**New Product Alerts:**
```
Track:
- New product launches
- Product discontinuations
- Range expansions
- Exclusive launches

Sources:
- Competitor websites
- Trade publications
- Social media
- Email newsletters
```

**Assortment Comparison:**
```
Analyze:
- Category depth
- Brand coverage
- Exclusive products
- Private label
- Gaps in their range
```

### Promotional Monitoring

**Promotion Tracking:**
```
Capture:
- Discount percentage/amount
- Promotion duration
- Products included
- Promo codes used
- Marketing channels

Frequency:
- Daily during major events
- Weekly regular monitoring
- Real-time for flash sales
```

- Content depth

**Gap Identification:**

- Better content opportunities
- Underserved queries

**Monitor:**
- Domain authority
- Total backlinks
- Referring domains
- New link acquisition
- Lost links

## SWOT Framework

### Template

```markdown
## Competitor SWOT: [Competitor Name]

### Strengths
- [What they do well]
- [Advantages they have]
- [Resources they have]

### Weaknesses
- [Where they fall short]
- [Gaps in offering]
- [Customer complaints]

### Opportunities (for us)
- [Where we can win]
- [Their weaknesses we can exploit]
- [Market gaps they're missing]

### Threats (to us)
- [What they could do to hurt us]
- [Their expansion plans]
- [Their competitive advantages]
```

### Key Questions

**Strengths:**
- What do customers praise them for?
- Where do they rank highest?
- What's their best-selling category?
- What unique features do they offer?

**Weaknesses:**
- What do customers complain about?
- What's missing from their range?
- Where are their prices uncompetitive?
- What's their service reputation?

**Opportunities:**
- What customer needs aren't they meeting?
- Where are they slow to respond?
- What markets are they ignoring?
- What trends are they missing?

**Threats:**
- What are they investing in?
- Are they expanding into our categories?
- What new partnerships do they have?
- How are they improving?
