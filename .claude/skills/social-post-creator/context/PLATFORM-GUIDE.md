# Platform Guide

## Instagram

### Feed Posts

**Specifications:**
- Caption: 2,200 characters max
- Hashtags: 30 max (5-15 recommended)
- Mentions: 20 max per caption
- Image: 1080x1080 (square), 1080x1350 (portrait)

**Best Practices:**
- Hook in first line (shows in preview)
- Use line breaks for readability
- Place hashtags at end or in comments
- Include call to action
- Use relevant emojis (not excessive)

**Algorithm Factors:**
- Saves and shares weighted heavily
- Comment engagement important
- Reels get priority reach
- Carousel posts perform well

### Stories

**Specifications:**
- Duration: 15 seconds max per story
- Text: Keep to ~100 characters visible
- Format: 9:16 vertical (1080x1920)
- Links: Available for all accounts

**Best Practices:**
- Use interactive stickers (polls, questions)
- Keep text brief and readable
- Use consistent brand fonts/colors
- Post consistently for algorithm
- Include clear CTAs

**Story Types:**
- Behind-the-scenes
- Quick tips
- Polls and questions
- Product highlights
- User content reshares

### Reels

**Specifications:**
- Duration: Up to 90 seconds
- Caption: 2,200 characters
- Aspect ratio: 9:16 (1080x1920)
- Cover image: 1080x1920

**Best Practices:**
- Hook in first 1-3 seconds
- Use trending audio when relevant
- Keep text on screen readable
- Add captions/subtitles
- Original content preferred

**Content Types:**
- How-to tutorials
- Before/after
- Day in the life
- Product demos
- Trending formats

## Facebook

### Feed Posts

**Specifications:**
- Text: 63,206 characters max
- Optimal: 40-80 characters for engagement
- Link posts: Best under 500 characters
- Images: 1200x630 recommended

**Best Practices:**
- Short posts get more engagement
- Questions drive comments
- Native video outperforms links
- Use Facebook-specific features
- Link in comments can help reach

**Post Types:**
- Text-only (surprisingly effective)
- Photo posts
- Video (native upload)
- Link shares
- Live video

### Stories

**Specifications:**
- Duration: 20 seconds max
- Format: 9:16 vertical
- Interactive elements available

**Best Practices:**
- Cross-post from Instagram
- Use for timely content
- Include interactive elements
- Keep text minimal

### Groups & Pages

**Page Posts:**
- Business-focused content
- Mix of content types
- Regular posting schedule
- Community engagement

**Group Posts:**
- Community discussion
- Value-first approach
- Less promotional
- Conversation starters

## LinkedIn

### Posts

**Specifications:**
- Character limit: 3,000
- Optimal: 150-300 characters
- Hashtags: 3-5 recommended
- Images: 1200x627 or 1080x1080

**Best Practices:**
- Professional tone
- Hook in first 2 lines
- Use line breaks strategically
- Tag relevant people/companies
- Comment to boost visibility

**Post Types:**
- Text-only (high engagement)
- Image posts
- Document carousels
- Video (native)
- Articles (long-form)

### Content That Works

**High Performers:**
- Personal stories with business lessons
- Industry insights and trends
- How-to and educational content
- Contrarian viewpoints
- Data and statistics

**Formatting:**
```
Hook line (stands alone)

[Line break]

Body content with value

→ Bullet points work well
→ Use arrows or symbols
→ Break up long text

[Line break]

Call to action + hashtags
```

| Platform | Preferred Content |
|----------|-------------------|

## Cross-Platform Strategy

### Repurposing Content

**Blog → Social:**
- Pull key points for posts
- Create carousel from sections
- Quote graphics
- Video summary

**Video → Multiple:**
- Full video on YouTube/FB
- Short clips for Reels/TikTok
- Quote cards from audio
- Blog post transcript

**Post → Stories:**
- Repurpose feed as story
- Behind-the-scenes of creation
- Polls related to topic
- Swipe up to full post

### Platform-Specific Tweaks

**Same Content, Different Execution:**
```
Instagram: Visual-first, emoji-rich, hashtag-heavy
Facebook: Shorter, more conversational, few/no hashtags
LinkedIn: Professional tone, industry relevance, 3-5 hashtags
Twitter: Concise, punchy, thread for depth
```

## Algorithm Tips

### Instagram

- Post Reels consistently
- Encourage saves and shares
- Reply to comments quickly
- Use all features (Stories, Reels, Feed)
- Authentic engagement matters

### Facebook

- Native content performs best
- Live video gets notification priority
- Groups have higher organic reach
- Meaningful interactions > reactions
- Avoid engagement bait

### LinkedIn

- Comment on others' posts
- Post consistently
- Text-only posts often outperform
- First hour engagement crucial
- Tag strategically (not spam)

## Posting Frequency

### Recommended Cadence

| Platform | Minimum | Optimal | Maximum |
|----------|---------|---------|---------|
| Instagram Feed | 3/week | 5-7/week | 2/day |
| Instagram Stories | Daily | Multiple daily | 7/day |
| Instagram Reels | 2/week | 4-7/week | 2/day |
| Facebook | 3/week | 5/week | 2/day |
| LinkedIn | 2/week | 3-5/week | 1/day |

### Quality vs Quantity

- Quality always beats quantity
- Consistency matters more than frequency
- Better to post less with better content
- Analyze what works for your audience
